[[Montreal, Quebec|Montreal]], [[Quebec]] |location_country = [[Canada]] |industry = [[Internet]], [[computer software]] |area served = {{flagicon|Earth}} Worldwide |homepage = [http://www.messagingarchitects.com www.messagingarchitects.com] }} '''Messaging Architects''' (MA) is a [[Canada|Canadian]] software company specializing in [[e-mail]] products. The flagship product is M+Platform, a product suite that prevents viruses, spam, and email abuse, ensures [[regulatory compliance]] and discovery, and supports disaster recovery. ==History== Launched in March 1995 as ChronoFAX, the company was a distributor of computer-based fax solutions. ChronoFAX became Tobit Software North America as result of a partnership with a German company called [[Tobit]] that produced fax software for the [[Novell]] [[NetWare]] and [[GroupWise]] markets. When the three-and-a-half-year partnership with Tobit came to a close, the company again renamed itself, as Messaging Architects. MA was initially a provider of add-ons for the Novell GroupWise messaging platform. Since then the company has launched other products, including: GWGuardian (now called M+Guardian) and GWArchive (M+Archive). The company aims to produce platform-independent products, to allow multiple email platforms co-exist in the same organization, and support bi-directional migration. Messaging Architects works with messaging platform providers to provide [[solution]]s for [[Microsoft Exchange Server|Microsoft Exchange]], Novell GroupWise, and [[Lotus Notes]]. The staff at Messaging Architects include [[Mark Crispin]], best known as inventor of the [[IMAP]] protocol, and GroupWise specialist Morris Blackham.http://www.panda.com/mrc/ ==Industry recognition== Messaging Architects' email security and email archiving solutions have been positioned in the [[Gartner]] [[Magic Quadrant]] reports. It also provides a system interoperability for organizations where both GroupWise and Exchange co-exist. M+Archive consolidates both GroupWise and Exchange archives into a single XML repository and provides robust search capabilities for ediscovery. * [http://www.messagingarchitects.com/products/m-guardian-email-security.html ''M+Guardian''] is a policy-based email security solution that integrates anti-malware, anti-spam, content filtering, and data leak prevention in a self-contained, fully-configured hardware appliance from Dell, or as a virtual appliance. * [http://www.messagingarchitects.com/products/m-securestore-email-storage.html ''M+SecureStore''] manages and stores the growing volume of corporate email.
