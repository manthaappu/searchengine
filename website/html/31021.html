{{primarysources|date=June 2010}} {{Memory types}} '''Reduced-latency Dynamic random access memory''' '''(RLDRAM)''' is a type of [[Random-access memory|random access memory]] developed by [[Micron Technology]]. RLDRAM memory is a low-latency, high-bandwidth [[DRAM]] designed for networking and [[L3 cache]], high-end commercial graphics, and other applications that require back-to-back READ/WRITE operations or completely random access.[http://www.micron.com/products/dram/rldram.html RLDRAM Memory: Unparalleled Bandwidth and Low Latency]. ==References== {{Reflist}} {{DRAM}} [[Category:Computer memory]]
