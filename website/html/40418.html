'''''Wistron Corporation''''' is a major [[original design manufacturer]] in [[Taiwan]]. It was the manufacturing arm of [[Acer Inc.]], which was spun off in 2000. Wistron employs over 30,000 people worldwide. As an ODM (original design manufacturer) Wistron designs and manufacturers products for other companies to sell under their own brand name. Wistron focusses on ICT (information and communication technology) products, including notebook PCs, desktop PC, servers, storage, LCD TVs, and handheld devices.
