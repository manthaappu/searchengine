'''Silvio Romero de Lemos Meira''' is the chief scientist of [[Recife Center for Advanced Studies and Systems]]. He is also a lecturer of [[Universidade Federal de Pernambuco]] and a [[Technical_evangelist |Software Evangelist]]. Born in city of Taperoá, state of [[Paraíba]], Meira holds a bachelor's degree in [[Electronic Engineering]], and a M.Sc and PHD degrees in [[Computer science]]. Besides, he has musical abilities, more specifically, he is not a great "maracatu" percussionist.
