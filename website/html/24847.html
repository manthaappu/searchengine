In [[linguistics]], the '''modal case''' ([[list of glossing abbreviations|abbreviated]] {{sc|'''mod'''}}) is a [[grammatical case]] used to express ability, intention, necessity, obligation, permission, possibility, etc. It takes the place of English [[modal verb]]s such as can, could, would, might, may.
