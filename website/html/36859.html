[[Image:Teledyne-Ryan-Firebee-hatzerim-1.jpg|thumb|250px|[[Ryan Firebee]] missile, used as a target drone.]] A '''target drone''' is an unmanned, [[remote control]]led aerial vehicle, usually used in the training of [[anti-aircraft]] crews[http://www.avonds.com/Target%20Drones.htm Avonds Scale Jets - Target Drones]. In their simplest form, target drones often resemble radio controlled model aircraft. More modern drones may use [[countermeasures]], [[radar]], and similar systems to mimic real aircraft[http://www.vectorsite.net/twuav_02.html Vector Site - Target Drones]. More advanced drones are made from large, old [[anti-ship missile]]s which had their warheads removed.
