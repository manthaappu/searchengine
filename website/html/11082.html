{{Multiple issues|orphan =September 2009|wikify =September 2009}} '''Draw a Secret''' (DAS) is a purely graphical [[password]] selection and input scheme. The scheme replaces [[alphanumeric]] password strings, with a simple picture drawn on a grid. '''Background Draw a Secret''' (BDAS), a variant of Draw a Secret, is purely graphical password selection and input scheme.
