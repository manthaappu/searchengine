The '''Impact Nominal Index''' or '''INI''' is a computer system that enables UK police forces to establish whether any other force holds information on a person of interest. It was created by the IMPACT Programme led by the [[Home Office]] in 2006. The system was built by the [[Web Technology Group]], a former subsidiary of [[Cable & Wireless]].
