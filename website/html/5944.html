The next year, he won $5,000 for cracking [[Safari_(web_browser)|Safari]] in under 10 seconds. In 2009 he also demonstrated an SMS processing vulnerability that allowed for complete compromise of the Apple [[iPhone]] and denial-of-service attacks on other phones.
