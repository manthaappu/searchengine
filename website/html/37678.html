{{orphan|date=March 2009}} '''''Throwim Way Leg''''' is a book written by Australian [[zoologist]] and [[climate change]] activist [[Tim Flannery]]. It documents Flannery's experiences in the highlands of [[Papua New Guinea]] and Indonesian [[Western New Guinea]]. The book describes the flora and fauna of the island and the cultures of the various tribes. The title is an anglicised spelling of the [[Tok Pisin|New Guinean Pidgin]] "Toromwe Lek." It is a syllabus choice for the HSC English Advanced course in NSW, Australia. {{science-book-stub}} [[Category:Science books]] [[Category:Australian books]] [[Category:Books by Tim Flannery]] [[Category:Western New Guinea]]
