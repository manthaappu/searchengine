{{advert|date=April 2010}} {{Infobox Website | name = ShareThis | logo = [[Image:ShareThis.png|ShareThis Logo]] | screenshot = | caption = | url = http://sharethis.com/ | type = Social Bookmarking and Sharing Tool | registration = Free | employees = 15 (2008) | owner = ShareThis | createdby = ShareThis }} The '''ShareThis''' button is an all-in-one [[GUI widget|widget]] that lets people share any content on the Web with friends via e-mail, AIM, or text message. The widget can also keep an account of personal contacts so that users can send an e-mail, IM, or text message with favorite links to friends. {{cite web|url = http://www.news.com/8301-10784_3-9892624-7.html|title = Publisher widget ShareThis raises $15 million, Source:CNET}} The button can be deployed on any site to drive traffic, stimulate viral activity, and track the sharing of content. Toolbar versions exist for Firefox, Mozilla Application Suite and Internet Explorer, and customized widgets are available for leading blog publisher sites including Blogger, TypePad and WordPress. == History == ShareThis was founded by Dr. [[David E. Goldberg]], engineering professor at [[University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign]], and Tim Schigel, then a Director at Blue Chip Venture Company (“Blue Chip”). ShareThis owns an exclusive license, with the University of Illinois, for Dr. Goldberg’s patent applications in the area of genetic algorithms and machine learning for the purposes of information discovery and learning from sharing behavior. ShareThis has headquarters in San Francisco, CA and Cincinnati, OH. Investors include [[IllinoisVENTURES]] and [[DFJ Mercury]]. ==Service details== ShareThis can be found on many popular blogs. ShareThis can also be used directly from a browser by download the ShareThis button. ShareThis allows users to import contacts from e.g. Gmail, Yahoo, Facebook, MySpace, AIM, and Outlook. A history is kept in the users personal ShareBox.
