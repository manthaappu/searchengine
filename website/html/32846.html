In [[computational fluid dynamics]] (CFD), '''SIMPLE algorithm''' is a widely used [[numerical algorithm|numerical procedure]] to solve the [[Navier-Stokes equation]]s. ''SIMPLE'' is an acronym for Semi-Implicit Method for Pressure Linked Equations. The SIMPLE algorithm was developed by Prof. [[Brian Spalding]] and his student [[Suhas Patankar|S V Patankar]] at [[Imperial College London|Imperial College]], London in the early 1970s.
