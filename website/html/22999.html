==MODAF Meta-Model== The '''MODAF Meta-Model''' (or M3, for short) is the underlying model for the [[UK Ministry of Defence|UK MoD]] Architecture Framework ([[MODAF]]). It defines a [[Unified Modeling Language|UML]] profile (abstract syntax only) by extending the UML 2.1 Meta-Model. The M3 was one of the key inputs to the [[Object Management Group|OMG]] [[UPDM]] specification. Version 1.1 of the M3 was released on 18 May 2007. The current version 1.2.004, was released in May 2010. ==External links== * [http://www.mod.uk/DefenceInternet/AboutDefence/CorporatePublications/InformationManagement/MODAF/ModafMetaModel.htm The official MODAF Meta-Model website] with downloads in [[Sparx]]; [[HTML]] and [[XMI]] formats.
