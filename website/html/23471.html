The '''Marine Life Information Network''' ('''MarLIN''') is an information system for [[marine biodiversity]] for [[Great Britain]] and [[Ireland]].[http://www.marlin.ac.uk/ MarLIN website] MarLIN was established in 1998 by the [[Marine Biological Association]] together with the environmental protection agencies and academic institutions in Britain and Ireland. The MarLIN data access programme has now become the DASSH Marine Data Archive Cantre. DASSH is built on the existing extensive data and dissemination skills of the Marine Life Information Network (MarLIN), the library expertise of the National Marine Biological Library (NMBL) and the MBA's historical role in marine science. ==References== {{reflist}} [[Category:Marine biology]] [[Category:Information systems]] [[Category:1998 establishments]]
