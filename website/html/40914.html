[[Image:Replace this image male.svg|right]] {{nihongo|'''Yoji Akao'''|赤尾 洋二|Akao Yōji|extra=born 1928}} is a [[Japan]]ese planning specialist recognized as the developer of [[Hoshin Kanri]] (a [[strategic planning]] methodology). With the late [[Shigeru Mizuno]], he developed [[Quality Function Deployment]] (a group [[decision making]] technique). Akao and Mizuno also co-founded the Quality Function Deployment Institute[http://www.qfdi.org/ QFD Institute. Home page of Quality Function Deployment Institute. The Official Source for QFD]: a non-profit organization dedicated to dissemination and advancement of QFD.
