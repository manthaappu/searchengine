'''History''' ---- [[Image:XGI Volari V3XT AGP.jpg|thumb|right|XGI Volari V3XT]] On September 15, 2003 [[XGI Technology]] Inc introduced the Volari V3. The V3 is a budget video card and is available with an AGP X8 interface from Walton Chaintech Corporation of Taiwan. The V3 is similar in performance but is generally cheaper than the ATI Radeon 9200 SE.
