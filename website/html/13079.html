'''FISINT''' stands for '''F'''oreign '''I'''nstrumentation '''S'''ignals '''INT'''elligence from the intercept of foreign electromagnetic emissions associated with the testing and operational deployment of non-US aerospace, surface, and subsurface systems." Typical examples of such communication include: *Telemetry data ([[TELINT]]). Missiles, satellites, and other remotely-monitored devices often transmit streams of data concerning their location, speed, engine status and other metrics. *Video data links.
