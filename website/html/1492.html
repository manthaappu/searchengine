'''Alisols''' define a soil group within the [[World Reference Base for Soil Resources]][[WRB]] ''[http://www.fao.org/docrep/W8594E/w8594e05.htm#chapter%202:%20key%20to%20the%20reference%20soil%20groups Ch2 Key to the reference soil groups]'' Properties include having an argic horizon, which has a specific [[cation exchange capacity]]; a predominantly alic properties zone between 250mm and 1000 mm from the soil surface; and no [[Soil horizon|diagnostic horizons]] other than an ochric, [[umbric]], albic, andic, ferric, nitic, plinthic or vertic horizon. ==References== {{reflist}} {{soil-sci-stub|acidification, soil}} {{Template:Soil type}} [[Category:Pedology]] [[Category:Classification systems]]
