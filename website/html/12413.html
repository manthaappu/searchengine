'''Eric Haines''' is an [[United States]] [[software engineer]], expert in [[computer graphics]], specifically in [[rendering (computer graphics)|image rendering]]. {{As of|2007|alt=Currently}} he is with [[Autodesk, Inc.]], lead [[software engineer]].
