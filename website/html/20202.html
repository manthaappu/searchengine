{{Italic title}} The '''''Journal of Computer-Mediated Communication''''' ({{ISSN|1083-6101}}) is a [[peer-reviewed]] [[academic journal]] that covers the [[interdisciplinary]] field of [[computer-mediated communication]]. It has been published since 1995 by [[Wiley-Blackwell]]. Since 2008, the [[editor-in-chief]] is Kevin Wright ([[University of Oklahoma]]).
