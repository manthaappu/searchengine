'''CA-Cricket Presents''' (formerly '''Xerox Presents''' originally from Xerox Desktop Software) was a [[presentation program]] developed for [[Apple Macintosh]] and [[Microsoft Windows]] by Cricket Software. Cricket Software was later acquired by [[Computer Associates]]. '''CA-Cricket Graph''' was an associated chart program. The program competed with [[PowerPoint]].
