'''Petname systems''' are naming systems that claim to possess all three naming properties - global, secure, and memorable {{cite web| url=http://www.skyhunter.com/marcs/petnames/IntroPetNames.html| title=An Introduction to Petname Systems }}. Software that uses such a system can satisfy all three requirements.
