The following are some examples of [[SHA-1]] and [[SHA-2]] digests. [[ASCII]] encoding is assumed for all messages. ==SHA-1 hashes== SHA1("[[The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog]]") = 2fd4e1c6 7a2d28fc ed849ee1 bb76e739 1b93eb12 Even a small change in the message will, with overwhelming probability, result in a completely different hash due to the [[avalanche effect]].
