NIST Special Publication 800-37, "Guide for Applying the Risk Management Framework to Federal Information Systems" was developed by the Joint Task Force Transformation Initiative Working Group. It aims to transform the traditional [[Certification and Accreditation]] (C&A) process into the six-step [[Risk management framework]] (RMF). The second step of the RMF is to select the appropriate subset of [[security controls]] from the control catalog in [[NIST Special Publication 800-53]]. ==External links== * [http://nistdocs.com NIST Special Publication 800-37 Guide for Applying the Risk Management Framework to Federal Information Systems] [[Category:Information assurance standards]] [[Category:Standards]]
