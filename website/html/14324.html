[[File:Fujistu iPAD.jpg|right|thumb|The Fujitsu iPAD]] The '''Fujitsu iPAD''' is a lightweight [[Handheld computing|handheld device]] that was introduced by [[Fujitsu]], in 2002. It runs Microsoft's [[Windows CE 5.0|CE.NET]] operating system. It supports [[802.11b]] wireless LAN to connect wirelessly with other company infrastructure.{{cite web|url=http://www.currentdirections.com/hardware/fujitsu/ipad100.html|title=Fujitsu IPad|accessdate=29 January 2010}} The device can support inventory management as well as credit card payments.{{cite web|url=http://www.fujitsu.com/us/services/retailing/technology/hardware/?navid=608|title=POS Hardware: Fujitsu United States|accessdate=29 January 2010}} In January 2010, when [[Apple inc|Apple]] announced the Apple [[iPad]], there was a naming controversy between the two devices.{{cite news|url=http://www.nytimes.com/2010/01/29/technology/companies/29name.html|title=IPad?
