'''Xia Li''' is a scientist based at the [[Monell Chemical Senses Center]] in [[Philadelphia]]. She is known for her work on feline sensory studies, including groundbreaking research as to why [[cat]]s cannot [[taste]] sweets. She, along with her collaborator Dr. [[Joseph Brand]], concluded that modern cats have lost function in an obsolete [[taste receptor]], removing the ability taste sweets, unlike their prehistoric ancestors.
