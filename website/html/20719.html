Kiwibox.com is a [[List of social networking websites|social networking]] destination and online magazine for young adults, with over half a million members.[http://www.kiwibox.com Kiwibox.com] Kiwibox is a social network with user profile pages, forums, blogs, and an online magazine. == Features == Kiwibox has a number of unique features such as offering member jobs, which include writing for the site's magazine content and advisor to other members. == History == [[File:Kiwibox 1999.png|left|thumb|Kiwibox 1.0, 1999 launch]] === 1999 - 2004 === Kiwibox was founded and launched.[http://www.reuters.com/article/pressRelease/idUS127724+04-Mar-2009+PRN20090304 Universal Music Group (UMG) and Kiwibox.com Launch Music Video Library] === 2005 - 2007 === Kiwibox exceeded 1 million registered members. Kiwibox merged with Magnitude Information Systems[http://www.redorbit.com/news/technology/851561/magnitude_announces_acquisition_agreement/index.html Magnitude Announces Acquisition Agreement] ([[OTC Bulletin Board|OTC BB]]:MAGY.OB). === 2008 - 2009 === August 2008, Kiwibox launched the new Kiwibox 2.0[http://www.adotas.com/2008/08/kiwibox-gives-teen-social-network-a-makeover/ Kiwibox Gives Teen Social Network a Makeover] site. With the launch of Kiwibox 2.0, the mobile platform also went live for users to gain access to Kiwibox via cell phones.[http://www.mobile-ent.biz/news/30841/Kiwibox-takes-its-community-into-mobile Kiwibox takes its community into mobile] Sadly, the mobile platform only allowed users to access a small part of the site and the link provided to view the full site was never functional. During 2008, the Kiwibox Teen Network[http://www.adotas.com/2009/03/kiwibox-burst-media-a-good-match/ Kiwibox, Burst Media, a good match] was also launched. In late 2008, Kiwibox signed contracts with Universal Music Group to distribute video content to its users.[http://www.adotas.com/2008/10/umg-to-distribute-music-videos-on-kiwibox/ UMG To Distribute Music Videos on Kiwibox] Early in 2009, with over 500 original self-produced videos, Kiwibox was accepted into the partnership program with [[youtube|YouTube, LLC]].[http://www.901am.com/2009/kiwibox-joins-youtube-partner-program.html Kiwibox Accepted Into YouTube Partner Program] Kiwibox crossed 2 million registered members. The 4KIWI [[SMS]] platform launched enabeling users to get [[SMS]] updates from news to friend alerts. === 2010 === During the beginning months of 2010, Kiwibox released their full functioning Android application allowing users to easily explore, connect, and party via their cellular phones. The revamped site is now a community for college students and young professionals, to connect with friends on the net and meet new people while having a fulfilling online experience. Kiwibox is a platform for them to show off their skills and interests, whether it is writing, photography, event promotion and planning, or general technical tasks.
