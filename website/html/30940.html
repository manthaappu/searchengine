'''R. Kent Dybvig''' is a professor of [[Computer Science]] at [[Indiana University Bloomington|Indiana University]] in [[Bloomington, Indiana]]. His research focuses on [[programming language]]s, and he is known in the [[Lisp (programming language)|Lisp]] community as the principal developer of the [[Chez Scheme]] compiler and run-time system. Together with [[Daniel P. Friedman]], he has long advocated the use of the [[Scheme (programming language)|Scheme]] language in teaching computer science.
