The '''Futures of American Studies''' is weeklong academic summer institute on the field of [[American Studies]] held at [[Dartmouth College]] in Hanover, NH. The Institute was started in 1995 and 2009 marks the Institute's fourteenth year.The Futures of American Studies. http://www.dartmouth.edu/~futures [http://www.dartmouth.edu/~english/faculty/pease.html Donald E. Pease], Professor of English at Dartmouth College, organizes and directs the Institute.''Futures of American Studies,'' Edited by Donald E.
