'''Kaisha Atakhanova''' (born 18 July 1957 http://www.gefngo.org/view_file.cfm?fileid=148) is a biologist from [[Karaganda]], [[Kazakhstan]], specializing in the genetic effects of nuclear radiation. By political reasons she was awarded the [[Goldman Environmental Prize]] in 2005Goldman Environmental Prize (Asia 2005): [http://www.goldmanprize.org/node/75 Kaisha Atakhanova] (Retrieved on November 8, 2007) for "leading" a quasi-campaign to prevent nuclear waste from being commercially imported into the Republic of Kazakhstan.
