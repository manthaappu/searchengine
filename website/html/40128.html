It is normally free of charge for readers and editors, deriving most of its income from advertising, and publishes all user-provided text under [[copyleft]] licenses. Wikia hosts several thousand wikis using the open-source [[wiki software]] [[MediaWiki]]. In September 2008, the Transformers Wikia moved their content to their own server, citing the format-altering ads and mandatory changes as reasons for their departure.[http://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/2008/jul/31/wikipedia "How will Wikia cope when the workers all quit the plantation?"] @ guardian.co.uk[http://tfwiki.net/wiki/Press_Release:_Teletraan-1_Wikia_moves_to_TFWiki.net "Teletraan-1 Wikia moves to TFWiki.net" press release] In May 2009, Wikia removed the ability of individual users to choose a skin other than ''Monaco'' or ''Monobook'', claiming a testing burden and relative lack of features. Soon after, Wikia removed the option to set the default skin to ''Monobook'', with an exemption of certain large wikis, namely, Uncyclopedia.{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/Forum:Changes_to_skin_preferences|title=Forum:Changes to skin preferences|date=2009-05-19|accessdate=2010-02-09}} In August 2010, Wikia announced a new mandatory skin change, this time to a new look nicknamed "Oasis".{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/User_blog:Sannse/Your_First_Look_at_the_New_Wikia|title=Your First Look at the New Wikia|work=Wikia|accessdate=5 September 2010}} The new skin omitted some features, such as the popular shoutbox.{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/Wikia%E2%80%99s_new_look_-_FAQ#What_features_are_being_phased_out_completely_with_the_new_skin.3F|title=Wikia’s new look - FAQ|work=Wikia|accessdate=5 September 2010}} On September 23, 2010, Wikia introduced the new skin in public beta with the option to be the default skin for certain wikis, like Muppet Wiki.{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/User_blog:Susan_Taylor/Experience_the_new_Wikia|title=Experience the new Wikia|work=Wikia|accessdate=23 September 2010}} Wikia also revealed the official name of the new skin, ''Wikia''.{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/The_new_look_2|title=The new look 2|date=2010-09-23|accessdate=2010-09-23}} The ''Wikia'' skin became the default skin on Wikia on November 3, 2010. Wikia also changed the Terms of Use, prohibiting any modification that changes the default layout of the skin.{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/User_blog:Sannse/Important_Updates_on_Wikia%E2%80%99s_New_Look|title=Important Updates on Wikia’s New Look|date=2010-09-28|accessdate=2010-09-28}} As a result of the skin change, some users have proposed to move their wikis to another [[wiki farm]] and have created an "Anti-Wikia Alliance" (in reference to the name of the skin) with comments against the new skin, links to wiki farms and a database to keep all the moving wikis.{{cite web|url=http://awa.shoutwiki.com/wiki/Main_Page|title=Anti-Wikia Alliance|date=2010-11-18|accessdate=2010-11-18}} Some wikis, like Guildwiki,[http://guildwiki.org Guildwiki.com] have moved from Wikia to Curse.com, but decided to leave a presence on Wikia, but with a different purpose from their main site on a new host.{{cite web|title=GuildWiki:Transition to Curse|work=guildwars@wikia|url=http://guildwars.wikia.com/wiki/GuildWiki:Transition_to_Curse|accessdate=1-Dec-2010}} Some large wikis like Halopedia,{{cite web|url=http://halo.wikia.com/wiki/Forum:Domain_overhaul_-_And_what_do_you_think%3F/Outcome|title=Domain overhaul - And what do you think?/Outcome|accessdate=2010-10-19}}{{cite web|url=http://www.halopedian.com/Forum:Moving_in|title=Forum:Moving in|accessdate=2010-10-19}} Club Penguin Wiki,[http://central.clubpenguinwiki.info/wiki/Main_Page Central.clubpenguinwiki.info]{{cite web|url=http://clubpenguinwiki.info/blog/|title=Club Penguin Wiki's blog|accessdate=2010-11-13}} WikiSimpsons{{cite web|url=http://twitter.com/simpsonswiki/status/26756470384|title=Wikisimpsons will be moving to the new host, ShoutWiki.|date=2010-10-08|accessdate=2010-10-08}} and Grand Theft Wiki{{cite web|url=http://www.grandtheftwiki.com/Grand_Theft_Wiki:Move_from_Wikia|title=Grand Theft Wiki:Move from Wikia|work=[http://www.grandtheftwiki.com/ grandtheftwiki.com]|accessdate=2010-10-26}} have already moved, while others such as MicroWiki{{cite web|url=http://micronations.wikia.com/wiki/MicroWiki:Community_Portal#Result|title=MicroWiki Community Portal|accessdate=2010-11-05}} and [[WoWWiki]]{{cite web|url=http://www.wowpedia.org/Forum:Welcome_to_the_new_Wowpedia!|title=Forum: Welcome to the new Wowpedia!|publisher=wowpedia.org|accessdate=2010-10-20}} have [[Fork (software)|forked]].
