{{BLP sources|date=July 2007}} '''Michael Sweet''' is a computer scientist and is the primary developer and architect of [[CUPS]], [[flPhoto]], [[HTMLDOC]], and [[Mini-XML]]. He was the original developer of the Gimp-Print software (now called [[Gutenprint]]) and contributes to other free software projects such as [[FLTK]], [[Newsd]], and [[Samba (software)|Samba]]. He owns and runs [[Easy Software Products]], a company that currently sells HTML publishing software. In February 2007, [[Apple Inc.]] hired Michael Sweet and purchased the CUPS source code.http://www.networkworld.com/news/2007/071307-apple-acquires-cups-printing.html"CUPS",[http://www.cups.org/articles.php?L475 CUPS Purchased by Apple Inc.] ([[press release]]), July 11, 2007. He continues to develop CUPS at Apple Inc.
