The '''Golden Realm RPG Engine''' is a [[Game Maker Language|GML]] based, easy-to-use [[Game engine|engine setup]] for creating both single player and [[Massively multiplayer online role-playing game|MMO RPG]] games on [[Microsoft Windows|Windows]] and [[Macintosh|Mac]]. It has been constantly edited and updated since its release in 2008. This engine can be found and purchased at [http://goldenrealmrpgengine.50webs.com/ http://goldenrealmrpgengine.50webs.com/].
