'''Boris Aronov''' is a computer scientist, currently a professor at [[Polytechnic University (New York)|Polytechnic University]]. His main area of research is [[computational geometry]]. He is a [[Sloan Research Fellow]]. Aronov earned his [[Bachelor of Arts|B.A.]] in [[computer science]] and [[mathematics]] in 1984 from [[Queens College, City University of New York]].
