The editors-in-chief are [[Stephen Grossberg]] (Cognitive and Neural Systems Dept., Boston University, USA), [[Kenji Doya]] (Initial Research Project, Okinawa Inst. Of Science & Tech., Japan) and [[John G. Taylor]] (Dept. of Mathematics, King's College, University of London, UK). '' Neural Networks'' is abstracted and indexed in [[Scopus]] and [[Science Citation Index]].
