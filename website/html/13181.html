'''Fabrik''' is a [[Visual programming language|visual]] [[Computer programming|programming]] [[integrated development environment]] implemented in [[Smalltalk]] and designed at [[Apple Computer]] by [[Dan Ingalls]], Scott Wallace, [[Yu-Ying Chow]], [[Frank Ludolph]], [[Ken Doyle]] and others during the mid-1980s.
