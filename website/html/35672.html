{{unreferenced|date=August 2010}} {{Infobox Company | name = Statsit | type = [[social media]] | foundation = 2008 | founder = Mikko Kotila, Juha Rantala | location_city = [[Kuala Lumpur]] | location_country = [[Malaysia]] | services = Social Media Monitoring | num_employees = 15 | homepage = http://www.statsit.com/ }} '''Statsit''' is a social media monitoring and insight company founded in 2008, based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The company specializes in measuring the effectiveness of marketing activities and providing brand insights via comments and conversations on social media sites including [[blogs]], [[forums]], [[Twitter]], [[Facebook]] and [[Digg]] to [[advertising agencies]].
