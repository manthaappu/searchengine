{{Unreferenced|date=April 2010}} The '''Hitachi SR8000''' is a high performance [[supercomputer]] manufactured by [[Hitachi Ltd.]]. This model of supercomputer is well known for its ability to process mathematical formulas to many digits of precision{{Citation needed|date=May 2010}}. It has 128 nodes, which is more than most other computers of this type{{Citation needed|date=May 2010}}. This type of supercomputer has performance that is beyond vector and parallel [[supercomputer]]s{{Citation needed|date=May 2010}}, since performance in mathematical calculations for those types of computing is limited{{Citation needed|date=May 2010}}. ==External links== * [http://www.hitachi.co.jp/Prod/comp/hpc/eng/sr81e.html Hitachi SR8000] == References == [[Category:Supercomputers]]
