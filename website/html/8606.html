In [[telecommunication]], a '''cryptochannel''' is a complete [[system]] of [[cryptography|crypto-communications]] between two or more holders. The basic unit for naval cryptographic communication. It includes: (a) the cryptographic aids prescribed; (b) the holders thereof; (c) the indicators or other means of identification; (d) the area or areas in which effective; (e) the special purpose, if any, for which provided; and (f) pertinent notes as to distribution, usage, ''etc.'' A cryptochannel is analogous to a [[radio]] [[Telecommunication circuit|circuit]]. ==See also== *[[Cryptosystem]] *[[Secure channel]] ==References== *{{FS1037C}} *{{DODDIC}} [[Category:Cryptography]] [[Category:Military communications]] {{telecomm-stub}}
