'''S-TEC Corporation''' is an operating company under the Avionics and Surveillance division of [[Cobham plc]].{{cite news |title=Cobham Buying S-Tec for $38 Million |url=http://www.ainonline.com/news/single-news-page/article/cobham-buying-s-tec-for-38-million/?no_cache=1&cHash=b89f4039f0 |work=AINonline |date=2007-11-27 |accessdate=2007-11-28}} S-TEC is a [[United States]] [[corporation]] originally founded in 1978{{cite web |url=http://www.meggitt.com/main.asp?folder=overview&Content=2 |title=Operating companies |accessdate=2007-03-29 |year=2006 |work=[[Meggitt|Meggitt PLC]]}} and headquartered in [[Mineral Wells, Texas]].{{cite web |url=http://www.s-tec.com/contact.html |title=CONTACT INFORMATION |accessdate=2007-03-29 |work=S-TEC}} They manufacture flight control systems for the General Aviation aftermarket as well as a number of [[original equipment manufacturers]].
