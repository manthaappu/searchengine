'''''Compliance Week''''' is a weekly newsletter and monthly magazine on [[corporate governance]] and [[United States]] [[regulatory compliance]] issues reaching more than 40,000 financial and legal executives at many U.S. [[public companies]]. Its editor, as of December 2010, is Edith Bentz.Compliance Week (magazine), December 2010 Anchored by columns and insights of numerous governance experts - including former [[U.S. SEC]] chairman [[Harvey Pitt]] - ''Compliance Week'' is published every Tuesday by Boston's [[Financial Media Holdings Group, Inc.]] Compliance Week provides news, best practices, checklists, primers, and other guidance to help its subscribers and their peers comprehend and comply with the latest regulations from the United States Securities and Exchange Commission. ==External links== * [http://www.complianceweek.com ''Compliance Week''] Compliance Week also hosts an annual conference every June, which brings together hundreds of compliance and corporate governance executives to review the most current best practices to comply with SOX and other governance laws. ==Endotes== [[Category:Newsletters]] [[Category:American business magazines]] [[Category:Regulatory compliance]] {{trade-mag-stub}}
