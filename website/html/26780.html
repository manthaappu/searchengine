{{multiple issues|context=October 2008|notability=October 2008|unencyclopedic=October 2008}} A '''null session''' is an anonymous connection to a freely accessible [[network share]] called [[IPC$]] on [[Microsoft Windows|Windows]]-based [[Server (computing)|server]]shttp://www.softheap.com/security/session-access.html. It allows immediate [[read and write access]] with [[Windows NT]]/[[Windows 2000|2000]] and read-access with [[Windows XP]] and [[Windows 2003|2003]]. The command to be inserted at a [[DOS]]-prompt is: net use \\[[IP address]]_or_[[host name]]\ipc$ "" "/user:"
