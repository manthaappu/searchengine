The company was founded in 1986 by Ajay Chopra, Mirek Jiricka and Randall Moore. The company completed its IPO (NASDAQ: PCLE) in November 1994. In 1997 Pinnacle purchased Miro Computer Systems GmbH and in 2001 the video editing business of FAST MultiMedia AG. In 2002 Pinnacle purchased [[VOB Computersysteme]] GmbH. In 2003 Pinnacle purchased [[Steinberg]], only to sell it off to [[Yamaha Corporation|Yamaha]] in 2004.
