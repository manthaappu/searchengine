[[File:Microsoft Forefront Logo.png|thumb|right|300px|Microsoft ForeFront logo]] '''Microsoft Forefront''' is a [[line of business | line-of-business]] security-oriented software application marketed by [[Microsoft Corporation]]. Microsoft Forefront products protect many business assets - including networks, network servers (such as [[Microsoft Exchange Server]] and [[Microsoft SharePoint Server]]) and individual devices.{{cite web|author=Microsoft|title=Microsoft Forefront Protection|url=http://www.microsoft.com/forefront/en/us/protect.aspx|accessdate=2009-09-29}} == Components == Forefront includes the following products:{{cite web|author=Microsoft|title=Microsoft Forefront FAQ|url=http://www.microsoft.com/forefront/en/us/faq.aspx|accessdate=2009-09-29}} ;[[Client (computing)|Client]] security * ''Forefront Endpoint Protection''[http://www.microsoft.com/forefront/endpoint-protection/en/us/product-overview.aspx Forefront Endpoint Protection] (formerly called ''Microsoft Forefront Client Security'' and ''Microsoft Client Protection''):[http://www.microsoft.com/forefront/clientsecurity/en/us/default.aspx ForeFront Client Security] An [[antivirus software]] product, Forefront Endpoint Protection is designed to protect business desktops and laptops as well as server operating-systems.
