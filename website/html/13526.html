{{Orphan|date=February 2009}} {{unreferenced|date=June 2007}} '''File binders''' are applications that allow a user to "bind" executables together resulting in a single executable. They are useful for [[Hacker (computer security)|crackers]] to insert other applications such as [[Trojan horse (computing)|trojan horse]] executables into otherwise harmless files, making them more difficult to detect.
