'''History''' ---- On September 15, 2003 [[XGI Technology]] Inc introduced the Volari V5. The V5 is a mainstream video card and is available with an AGP X8 interface from Walton Chaintech Corporation of Taiwan. It is similar in terms of clock speed to the Radeon 9600 PRO and the Geforce FX 5600.
