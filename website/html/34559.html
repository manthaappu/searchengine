'''Siri''' is a [[personal assistant]] application for the [[iPhone OS]]. The application uses [[natural language processing]] to answer questions, make recommendations, and perform actions by delegating requests to an expanding set of [[web services]]. The iPhone app is the first public product by its makers, who are focused on [[artificial intelligence]] applications. Siri's marketing claims include that Siri adapts to the user's individual preferences over time and personalizes results, as well as accomplishing tasks such as making dinner reservations and reserving a cab. [http://www.siri.com/about/ Siri homepage] ==Funding== Siri was founded in December 2007 by Dag Kittlaus (CEO), Adam Cheyer (VP Engineering), and Tom Gruber (CTO/VP Design), together with Norman Winarsky from SRI's venture group. On October 13, 2008, Siri announced it had raised an $8.5 million Series A financing round, led by [[Menlo Ventures]] and [[Morgenthaler Ventures]].
