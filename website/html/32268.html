'''Robonexus''' is the largest [[robotics]] event in the [[United States|U.S.]]. Over 10,000 Attended RoboNexus in 2004. Another event is scheduled for 2005 in [[Silicon Valley]]. Robonexus is an event that showcases advances in robotics for both industry representatives and the general public.
