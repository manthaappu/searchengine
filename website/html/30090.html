{{Unreferenced|date=November 2009}} Process Supervision is a form of [[Operating system service management]] in which some master process remains the parent of the service processes. ==Benefits== Benefits include: * Ability to restart services which have failed * The fact that it does not require the use of "pidfiles" * [http://smarden.org/runit/benefits.html#state Clean process state] * Reliable logging, because the master process can capture the stdout/stderr of the service process and route it to a log * Faster (concurrent) and ability to start up and stop ==Implementations== * [[runit]] * [[Initng]] [[Category:Computing terminology]]
