{{Expert-subject|Physics|date=September 2008}} {{Expert-subject|Medicine|date=September 2008}} '''Magnetogastrography''' is the science of recording magnetogastrograms (MGGs). Magnetogastrograms are recordings of magnetic fields resulting from electrical activity from the stomach and can be considered similar to [[electrocardiograms]]. The magnetic fields are typically recorded using [[SQUID]]s. == See also ==
