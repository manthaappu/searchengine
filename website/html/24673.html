{{Unreferenced|date=December 2009}} {{Orphan|date=December 2009}} '''Minimum mode''' is one of two different [[hardware mode]]s of the [[Intel 8086]] and [[Intel 8088|8088]] processors ([[Central processing unit|CPU]]). The other is [[maximum mode]]. Mode selection is accomplished by how the chip is hard-wired in the circuit. Specifically, pin #33 (MN/MX) is used to select the mode, depending on whether it is wired to voltage or to ground. Changing the state of pin #33 changes the function of certain other pins. Mode cannot be changed by software. [[Category:x86 architecture]]
