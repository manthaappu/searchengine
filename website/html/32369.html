[[Image:Rodovid logo.png|140px|right|thumb|The Rodovid logo]] '''Rodovid''' is a free online collaborative [[family tree]] portal. Originally a Ukrainian project, as of August 2009 it has active communities in over 19 languages.{{ua icon}} [http://life.pravda.com.ua/private/4ac3264566836/ ''Pravda Life'' article], September 30, 2009. Retrieved October 7, 2009 It provides a [[web service]] built using [[MediaWiki]] and its own Rodovid Engine software to help store and visualize family relationships.[http://engine.rodovid.org/wk/Main_Page Discussion about the engine] from engine.rodovid.org. == About the project == Rodovid was founded in September 2005 by a group of developers and genealogy enthusiasts in [[Kiev]], [[Ukraine]].[http://en.rodovid.org/wk/Credits Credits page] The name Rodovid comes from the Ukrainian word Rodovid, meaning "lineage" or "genealogy". As of July 2009, Rodovid had over 320,000 total records for individuals and families across all languages, including 65,000 in each of English and Russian.{{en icon}} [http://en.rodovid.org/wk/Rodovid_Statistics The Rodovid statistics page][http://en.rodovid.org/wk/Main_Page Rodovid.org English Main Page], retrieved July 31, 2009 [http://ru.rodovid.org/wk/Многоязычное_генеалогическое_древо Russian Main Page], retrieved October 7, 2009] The Rodovid project's data and descriptions are stored on servers in Kiev, and available under the [[Creative Commons Attribution]] license where not already in the public domain. It has experimented with supporting imports of [[GEDCOM]] files.
