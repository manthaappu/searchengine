'''Mary Shaw''' (born 1943) is an American [[software engineer]], and the [[Alan J. Perlis]] Professor of Computer Science in the School of Computer Science at [[Carnegie Mellon University]], Pittsburgh, United States. Shaw's main area of research interest is [[software engineering]], including [[software architecture|architectural]], educational and historical aspects. == Selected publications == Mary Shaw has published several articles{{dblp name|id=s/Shaw:Mary|name=Mary Shaw}} and books. ;Books * 1967.
