It was developed by the [[Junkbusters Corporation]] and released under the [[GNU General Public License]]. However, the Internet Junkbuster hasn't been updated since 1998 and the junkbusters.com web site is no longer maintained. [[Privoxy]] is an up-to-date filtering proxy based on the Junkbuster code, but it is much more powerful.
