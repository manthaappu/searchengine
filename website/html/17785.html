'''Ian Colin Graham Bell''' (born 31 October 1962 in [[Hatfield, Hertfordshire|Hatfield]], Hertfordshire) is a [[game programmer]], [[game designer]] and [[game producer]]. He is most famous for co-authoring the computer game ''[[Elite (computer game)|Elite]]'' (1984). He attended the independent [[St Albans School (Hertfordshire)|St Albans School]]. He studied at [[Jesus College, Cambridge]], graduating with a degree (1st) in [[Mathematics]] in 1985, and a [[Cambridge Diploma in Computer Science]] in 1986.
