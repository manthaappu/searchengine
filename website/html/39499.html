{{Unreferenced stub|auto=yes|date=December 2009}} In [[computer]] technology, a '''virtual address''' is an address identifying a virtual, i.e. non-physical, entity. [[Image:Virtual address space and physical address space relationship.svg|thumb|300px]] ==Description== The term ''virtual address'' is most commonly used for an address pointing to [[virtual memory]] or, in networking, when referring to a virtual network address.
