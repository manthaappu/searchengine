'''SaveFace''' is a [[bookmarklet]] utility that, when invoked, automatically changes [[Facebook]] users' privacy settings to Friends Only. It is a free utility, developed by [[Untangle]], Inc. {{cite news | title = Untangle launches SaveFace | url = http://www.untangle.com/About-Us/Untangle-launches-SaveFace | publisher = Untangle, Inc.| date = 2010-05-17| accessdate = 2010-05-20}} It was launched on May 17, 2010, amid growing reports of Facebook changes to default privacy settings for Facebook users. {{cite news | title = Quick Fix Promises To Ensure Facebook Privacy | url = http://cbs5.com/technology/facebook.privacy.settings.2.1699404.html | publisher = CBS Broadcasting Inc.| date = 2010-05-17| accessdate = 2010-05-20 }} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}{{cite news | title = 4 tools to help reclaim Facebook privacy | url = http://scitech.blogs.cnn.com/2010/05/18/4-tools-to-help-reclaim-facebook-privacy/ | publisher = CNN.com| first=Stephanie| last=Goldberg|date = 2010-05-18| accessdate = 2010-05-20}}{{cite news | title = New Facebook Applications designed to easily reset member's privacy settings | url = http://www.hotindienews.com/2010/05/18/1023763/ | publisher = Hot Indie News| date = 2010-05-18| accessdate = 2010-05-20}}{{cite news | title = Facebook App SaveFace Gives Users Back Their Privacy | url = http://www.itproportal.com/portal/news/article/2010/5/19/facebook-app-saveface-gives-users-back-their-privacy/ | publisher = IT Pro| first=James| last=Cullimore| date = 2010-05-17| accessdate = 2010-05-20}} ==References== {{reflist}} ==External links== * [http://www.untangle.com/saveface SaveFace] [[Category:Facebook applications]]
