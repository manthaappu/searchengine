{{notable|date=September 2010}} The '''Conference on Semantics in Healthcare and Life Sciences''' ('''CSHALS''') is a scientific meeting on the practical applications of [[semantic technology]] to pharmaceutical R&D, healthcare, and life sciences. It has been held annually since 2008, and is the premier meeting in this domain.
