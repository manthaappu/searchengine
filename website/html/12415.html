'''Eric Lengyel''' is a [[computer game]] engine developer with expertise in 3D graphics, animation, physics, audio, and networking. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the [[University of California, Davis]] and a Masters Degree in Mathematics from [[Virginia Tech]]. He is the author of the textbook ''Mathematics for 3D Game Programming and Computer Graphics'' ([http://www.charlesriver.com/ Charles River Media], 2002), and he is the editor for the ''Game Engine Gems'' series of books. He is also a member of the editorial board for the ''[[Journal of Graphics Tools]]'' and a contributor to the older ''Game Programming Gems'' series of books. Dr. Lengyel founded [http://www.terathon.com/ Terathon Software] in 2000 and is currently President and Chief Technology Officer at the company, where he heads up programming for the [[C4 Engine]]. He has previously worked in the advanced technology group at [[Naughty Dog]], and before that was the lead programmer for the fifth installment of [[Sierra Online|Sierra's]] popular RPG adventure series ''[[Quest for Glory]]''. Eric Lengyel is originally from [[Reynoldsburg, Ohio]], but now lives in [[Roseville, California]].
