{{Infobox Person | name = Harald Tveit Alvestrand | image = Alvestrand-20071102.jpg | image_size = | caption = | birth_date = {{Birth date and age|1959|6|29|mf=y}} | birth_place = [[Namsos]], [[Norway]] | death_date = | death_place = | occupation = [[computer scientist]] | spouse = | parents = | children = 3 }} '''Harald Tveit Alvestrand''' (born 29 June 1959) is a [[Norway|Norwegian]] [[computer scientist]]. He was the chairman of the [[Internet Engineering Task Force]] from 2001 until 2005. He is an author of several important [[Request for Comments|RFC]]s, many in the general area of [[Internationalization and localization]].{{cite web |url=http://www.arkko.com/tools/allstats/haraldalvestrand.html |title=RFCs and current [[IETF]] activities by Harald T. Alvestrand |author=Jari Arkko |accessdate=2008-06-14 }} He was born in [[Namsos]], [[Norway]], received his education from [[Bergen Katedralskole]] and the [[Norwegian Institute of Technology]], and has worked for [[Norsk Data]], [[UNINETT]], EDB Maxware and [[Cisco Systems, Inc.]]. He currently (2008) lives in [[Trondheim]], [[Norway]], and works for [[Google]]. At the end of 2007 Harald Alvestrand was selected for the [[ICANN]] Board.{{cite web |url=http://www.icann.org/biog/alvestrand.htm |title=Biographical Data on Harald Tveit Alvestrand |year=2008 |accessdate=2008-06-14 |publisher=ICANN}} In 2001 he became a member of the [[Unicode Consortium|Unicode Board of Directors]].{{cite web |url=http://unicode.org/consortium/directors.html |title=Unicode Directors, Officers, and Staff |year=2008 |accessdate=2006-06-14 |publisher=[[Unicode Consortium]] }} He has more recently been a co-Chair of the [[IETF]] EAI{{cite web |url=http://tools.ietf.org/wg/eai |title=Email Address Internationalization |year=2008 |publisher=IETF WG |accessdate=2008-06-14}} and USEFOR{{cite web |url=http://tools.ietf.org/wg/usefor |title=Usenet Article Standard Update |year=2007 |publisher=IETF WG |accessdate=2008-06-14}} WGs. Harald Alvestrand is the Executive Director of the ''[[Linux Counter]] Organization''.{{cite web|url=http://counter.li.org/organization/board.php |title=Linux Counter Organization: Board
