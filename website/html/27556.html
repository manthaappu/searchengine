'''Open individualism''' is the view in philosophy according to which there exists only one [[Identity (philosophy)|numerically identical]] subject who is everyone.{{cite book | last = Kolak | first = Daniel | title = I Am You: The Metaphysical Foundations for Global Ethics | publisher = Springer | year = 2005 | isbn = 1402029993}} It is a theoretical solution for the question of [[Personal identity (philosophy)|personal identity]].
