{{Orphan|date=February 2009}} '''TBIL''' is an abbreviation for '''Tiny BASIC Interpreter Language'''. The TBIL Data Converter is a handy tool for the quick and effective [[transliteration]] between data in [[font]]/[[ASCII]]/[[Roman alphabet|Roman]] format in Office documents into a [[Unicode]] form in any of 7 [[Microsoft]]-supported [[India]]n languages.
