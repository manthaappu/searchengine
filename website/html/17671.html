{{lowercase|title=iRobot Negotiator}} The '''iRobot Negotiator''' is an unmanned robotic platform from the [[iRobot| iRobot Corporation]]. The 34 lb (15.4 kg) robot designed for use in civil response operations by civil defense professionals such as [[SWAT]] teams, police officers, etc. iRobot Negotiator performs basic [[reconnaissance]], thus increasing situational awareness.
