'''OBJ3''' is a version of [[OBJ (programming language)|OBJ]] based on [[Order theory|order]]-sorted [[rewriting]]. OBJ3 is [[Intelligent agent|agent]]-oriented and runs on [[Kyoto Common Lisp]] AKCL.
