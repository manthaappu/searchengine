The '''overlap coefficient''' is a similarity measure related to the [[Jaccard index]] that computes the overlap between two sets which is defined as follows: :\mathrm{overlap}(X,Y) = \frac{| X \cap Y | }{\min(|X|,|Y|)} If set ''X'' is a subset of ''Y'' or the converse then the overlap coefficient is equal to one. ==References== * [http://www.dcs.shef.ac.uk/~sam/stringmetrics.html Similarity Metrics], University of Sheffield, Department of Computer Science [[Category:Information retrieval]] [[Category:String similarity measures]] [[Category:Measure theory]]
