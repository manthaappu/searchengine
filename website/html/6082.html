A '''chief programmer team''' is a [[programming team]] which is organised around a [[chief programmer]] who is an expert [[programmer]]. The other team members have other, specialised roles, such as [[librarian]], which support the chief programmer in his primary task of designing and coding the [[software]].{{citation |url=http://books.google.co.uk/books?id=oMN1SR7KQd0C&pg=PA352 |title=Software engineering for students: a programming approach |author=Doug Bell}} The concept is similar to that of a [[surgical team]] in which a [[surgeon]] who performs the [[Surgery|operation]] is supported by medical staff such as an [[anaesthetist]] and [[nurse]]s. ==References== {{reflist}} {{compu-prog-stub}} [[Category:Software project management]]
