{{Orphan|date=August 2009}} {{Notability|Web|date=April 2010}} {{Primary sources|date=April 2010}} '''Cross.tv''' is a christian social networking website, similar to [[facebook]] and [[myspace]] (with the exception that it is based on religious faith, unlike the two previously mentioned networks), founded by the Cross media group.http://www.cross.tv The site provides users with facilities to upload and share pictures, audios, and videos. Users can also write blogs and create forums in this multi-lingual supportive website.
