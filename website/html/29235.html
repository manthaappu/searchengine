[[Image:Picower Institute-20060913.jpg|thumb|Picower Institute reflecting the [[Stata Center]]]] The '''Picower Institute for Learning and Memory''' is, along with the [[McGovern Institute for Brain Research]] and the Department of Brain and [[Cognitive Science]]s, one of the three [[neuroscience]] groups at [[MIT]]. The institute is focused on studying all aspects of learning and memory; specifically, it has received over [[US$]]50 million to study [[Alzheimer's]], [[schizophrenia]] and similar diseases. When it was established in 1994, the institute was primarily funded by the [[Sherman Fairchild Foundation]], the [[RIKEN Brain Science Institute]] and the [[National Institute of Mental Health]]. It was renamed after a massive grant by the [[Jeffry Picower#Involvement with Bernard Madoff|Picower Foundation]] in 2002, the eponymous charity group funded by profits from [[Bernard Madoff|Bernard Madoff's ponzi scheme]].{{cite web|title=About|url=http://web.mit.edu/picower/about/index.html|publisher=Massachusetts Institute of Technology|accessdate=2009-10-25}} On July 1, 2009, Professor Li-Huei Tsai became the director of the Picower Institute.{{cite web|title=Li-Huei Tsai to direct Picower Institute|url=http://web.mit.edu/newsoffice/2009/picower-0612.html|publisher=Massachusetts Institute of Technology|accessdate=2010-3-3}} The institute was directed by founder and [[Nobel Prize]] laureate [[Susumu Tonegawa]] until he resigned on December 31, 2006, motivated by his belief that “a new generation of leadership is needed.”{{cite news|author=Wang, Angeline|title=Director of Picower to Step Down|url=http://tech.mit.edu/V126/PDF/N55.pdf|format=PDF||work=The Tech|publisher=Massachusetts Institute of Technology|date=November 21, 2006|volume=126|issue=55|accessdate=2009-10-25}} ==Notes== {{reflist}} ==External links== *[http://web.mit.edu/picower/about/index.html Official website] {{MITtemplate}} {{coord missing|United States}} [[Category:Massachusetts Institute of Technology]] [[Category:Cognitive science research institutes]] [[Category:Neuroscience research centers]] [[Category:Research institutes in the United States]] {{US-northeast-university-stub}} {{neuroscience-stub}}
