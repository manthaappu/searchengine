{{Notability|date=October 2009}} The '''proximative case''' is used to describe a meaning similar to that of the English preposition "near to" or "close to". It is used in the [[logical language]] "Gimív". An example of its use is ‘basúnid’ which the creator has given to mean ‘near a school.’ ==Sources== *http://www.angelfire.com/fang/gimiv/Homepage.html {{Grammatical cases}} [[Category:Grammatical cases]] {{ling-stub}} [[br:Troad proksimativel]]
