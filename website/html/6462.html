'''J.C. (Cliff) Shaw''' was a systems programmer at the [[RAND Corporation]]. He is a coauthor of the first [[artificial intelligence]] program, the [[Logic Theorist]], and was one of the developers of [[Information Processing Language]], a [[programming language]] of the 1950s.
