It contains 656 students from the ages of 11 to 16.{{cite web|url=http://www.ofsted.gov.uk/oxedu_providers/full/(urn)/123865|title=Ansford School|publisher=Ofsted|accessdate=19 November 2010}} It is run by the head teacher Robert Benzie. The main school was built in 1940 with additional rooms for science, technology, mathematics and modern languages being added in the 1970's. A new £1.7 million Sports Centre was completed in 2005.{{cite web|url=http://www.ansford.somerset.sch.uk/facilities.html|title=Ansford School Facilities|publisher=Ansford School|accessdate=2009-01-23}} The school has its own animated micro-series set around the fictional deaths of Mr Kershaw, a teacher at the school and has cameos from other school teachers as well including headmaster Mr Benzie. It was created by former student Callum Dower who has now departed from the school. The series is named ″Who killed Mr Kershaw?″. Kevin Cruise, from series four of Britain's got talent, attended Ansford School. It is a great school with quality teachers.
