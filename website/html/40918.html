'''Yoram Moses''' ({{lang-he|יוֹרָם מוֹזֶס}}) is a Professor in the [[Electrical Engineering]] Department at the [[Technion - Israel Institute of Technology]]. Yoram Moses received a B.Sc. in mathematics from the [[Hebrew University of Jerusalem]] in 1981, and a Ph.D. in [[Computer Science]] from [[Stanford University]] in 1986. Moses is a co-author of the book ''Reasoning About Knowledge'', and is a winner of the 1997 [[Gödel Prize]] in theoretical computer science and the 2009 [[Dijkstra Prize]] in Distributed Computing.
