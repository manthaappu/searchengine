{{otherpersons|Tom Miller}} '''Tom Miller''' (born in 1950) is a [[software developer]] who is employed by [[Microsoft]]. Miller worked as a member of the original team of developers who followed [[Dave Cutler]] from [[Digital Equipment Corporation|DEC]] to Microsoft, where he initially started working in the networking group. After less than two years, Miller moved to the [[Windows NT]] team, where he worked with [[Gary Kimura]] on file systems and wrote the original 50 page specification document for the [[NTFS|NT File System]]. {{cite book | title=Showstopper!
