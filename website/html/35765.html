[[BPM]] |prizes = |religion = |footnotes = }} '''Steve Nimmons''', FBCS, FRI, FRSA, FIMIS, FIAP, FLS, FSA Scot, is a senior Information Technologist and industry commentator. minds'.[http://www.atosorigin-ebusiness.com/en-us/Business_Insights/innovation/scientific_community/default.htm Atos Origin Scientific Community][http://blog.atosorigin.com/author/steve-nimmons/ Atos
