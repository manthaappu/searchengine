In [[computing]], the '''KOMPILER''' was one of the first language [[compiler|compilation]] and runtime systems for [[International Business Machines]]' [[IBM 701]], the fastest commercial [[United States|U.S.]] [[computer]] available in 1955. Information on KOMPILER is listed on page 16 of Volume 2, Number 5 (May 1959) of the ''[[Communications of the ACM]]''. Known versions are KOMPILER 2 for IBM 701 and KOMPILER 3 for the [[IBM 704]].
