{{for|the actual London street|Old Street}} {{Cleanup|date=June 2009}} [[File:Old Street Landmark.jpg|thumb|200px|right|Urban sculpture / advertising hording in the centre of Old Street roundabout. From the south side of the roundabout, looking north.]] '''Old Street Roundabout''' is located on the boundary of the [[London Borough of Hackney]] and the [[London Borough of Islington]] and is an interchange system linking [[City of London]], west [[London]], east London and [[North London]]. The area surrounding the roundabout is also known as the ''Silicon Roundabout'' due to the prominence of British web based companies. ===South=== This direction towards [[City of London]] is City Road heading southbound towards the [[Square Mile]]. In this direction you are also heading towards [[Moorgate]] and [[Liverpool Street]] Stations. This area is in the [[London]] [[Congestion Charge]] zone. Further beyond you can end up in [[South London]] if you follow a more southern direction from the [[Square Mile]] across the [[River Thames]]. ===West=== This direction heads towards west [[London]] is Old Street westbound which goes towards the [[West End of London|West End]] and goes into the main Central London District and enters the [[London Borough of Camden]]. This direction passes [[Holborn]] and [[Tottenham Court Road]] and [[Oxford Street]]. It also comes across [[London]] [[Chinatown]]. Beyond it goes into the more expensive areas of [[London]], [[Chelsea, London|Chelsea]] and [[Kensington]] passing landmarks such as [[Natural History Museum]] and also [[Heathrow Airport]]. ===East=== This direction heads towards east London is Old Street eastbound which goes towards the [[East End]] and into the [[London Borough of Hackney]]. This direction goes towards [[Shoreditch]] and [[Dalston]]. It also comes across the famous [[Asia]]n street known as [[Brick Lane]] which is famous for its culture and its centre, [[Banglatown]]. Further beyond from here you can reach [[Lower Clapton|Clapton]], [[Leyton]] and [[Walthamstow]]. The local airport in East London is [[London City Airport]] for short haul [[Europe]]an flights, not far from Old Street Roundabout. ===North=== [[Image:Old St Rdbt.jpg|thumb|Old Street Roundabout in the direction towards Angel, Holloway and King's Cross in North London]] This direction heads towards [[North London]] is City Road Northbound. In this direction you are heading towards [[Angel]], [[London King's Cross railway station|King's Cross]], [[St Pancras railway station|St Pancras]], [[Euston Road|Euston]] or just off the [[A1200]] route head towards [[Holloway, London|Holloway]] and [[Archway, London|Archway]] which is considered as the link road between [[London]] and the North of the country and therefore from [[Highbury Corner]] is known as the A1. Beyond this point you can end up heading towards [[Paddington]], [[Barnet]] and [[Wembley]] depending if you use the A501 route route or A1200 route from Old Street Northbound. ==Old Street station== [[Old Street Station]] is below Old Street Roundabout and runs the [[London Underground]] and consists of the [[Northern Line]] and a [[First Capital Connect]] Service. ==Silicon Roundabout== The [[roundabout]] at the junction of [[City Road]] and [[Old Street]] in [[London]] is known as '''Silicon Roundabout''', due to the high number of web businesses located in the immediate area. This is named after the [[Silicon Valley]] area, of the West coast of the [[United States]], where there is a high concentration of influential web companies.
