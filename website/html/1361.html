{{Cleanup|date=May 2007}}{{Like resume|date=December 2007}} '''Aleksandar Lazarevic''' (born in [[Krusevac]], [[Serbia]]) is a renowned computer scientist who specializes in research on [[Data Mining]]. In addition, his research interests include security applications, video surveillance, parallel and distributed computing. He earned B.Sc and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering from the [[University of Belgrade Faculty of Electrical Engineering]] in 1994 and 1997 respectively. In 1997, he went to United States to start his doctoral studies. In 2001, he received the PhD degree in Computer and Information Science from [[Temple University]] in Philadelphia. Dr Lazarevic has an experience in both academic and industrial environment. He works as a Senior Research Scientist at [http://www.utrc.utc.com United Technologies Research Center], in Connecticut and as a Research Associate at the Army High Performance Computing Research Center at [http://www.it.umn.edu/ Institute of Technology], [[University of Minnesota]].
