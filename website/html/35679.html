{{Infobox Convention | name = Steamcon® | image = [[File:Steamconlogo.pdf|thumb|alt=Alt text|Steamcon Logo]] | caption = | status = Active | genre = [[Steampunk]] | venue = Seattle Airport Marriott and Hilton | location = {{flagicon|Washington}} [[SeaTac, Washington|Sea-Tac]], Washington | country = {{USA}} | first = | last = | organizer = | filing = | attendance = | website = http://www.steamcon.org/ }} '''Steamcon'''® is one of the largest regional [[steampunk]] conventions/[[symposium]] in the [[United States]]. Located in the [[Seattle]] area of [[Washington (U.S. state)|Washington]] state, Steamcon® held its first symposium in 2009. ==Overview== Steamcon® grew out of the desire of its founders to host a [[steampunk]] event in greater [[Seattle]]. The ideahttp://www.tor.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=blog&id=55345 "Interview with Steamcon Vice Chair Diana Vick(2009)" (Tor.com) to start the event was hatched at the local science fiction convention [[Norwescon]] in 2008 when multiple people expressed an interesthttp://www.seattle-geekly.com/?p=3859 "Audio Interview of Steamcon Vice Chair Diana Vick(2009)" (SeattleGeekly.com) to hold an event devoted exclusively to steampunk. ==Current and upcoming symposia== ===2010=== The second annual Steamcon® was held November 19th - 21st, 2010. The host venues were the Hilton Seattle Airport Hotel and the Seattle Airport Marriott. '''Guests of Honor''' * Author Guest of Honor: [[James Blaylock]] * Artist Guest of Honor: Jake Von Slatt * Games Guest of Honor: [[Shane Hensley]] * Musical Guests of Honor: [[Abney Park (band)|Abney Park]] ===2011=== The third annual Steamcon® will be held October 14th - 16th, 2011. The venue will be the Hyatt Regency Bellevue. ==Past Symposiums== ===2009=== The firsthttp://www.locusmag.com/2009/2009Conventions.htmlhttp://upcoming.yahoo.com/event/1491011/WA/Seattle/Steamcon/Seattle-Airport-Marriott Steamcon® was held October 23-25, 2009http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/outdoors/2010108738_nwwdatebook22.html Seattle Times Newspaper Listing at the Seattle Airport [[Marriott]], [[Seattle]], Washington. The convention in its first year had 1,350 people attend, making it one of the largest, if not the largest, steampunk devoted conventions in North America.
