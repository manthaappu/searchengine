'''Language engineering''' is the creation of [[natural language processing]] systems whose cost and outputs are measurable and predictable as well as establishment of language regulators, such as formal or informal agencies, committees, societies or academies as language regulators. Although this is usually referred to as [[language planning]], the word [[engineering]] is used to refer specifically to linguistic design or developing new structures to meet contemporary needs.
