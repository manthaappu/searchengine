'''William Fetter''' (1928-2002) was an American [[computer graphics]] art director. In 1964, while working for [[Boeing]], he made the first computer model of a human body ("[[Boeing Man]]"). He coined the term ''Computer Graphics'' in 1960, to describe his work at Boeing.
