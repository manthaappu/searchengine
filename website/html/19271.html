Inventory control - supervision of supply, storage and accessibility of items in order to insure an adequate supply without excessive oversupply. It can also be referred as internal control - an accounting procedure or system designed to promote efficiency or assure the implementation of a policy or safeguard assets or avoid fraud and error etc. '''Inventory control''' can refer to several concepts: *In [[economics]], the [[inventory control problem]], which aims to reduce overhead cost without hurting sales. *In the field of [[loss prevention]], systems designed to introduce technical barriers to [[shoplifting]]. It answers the 3 basic questions of any supply chain: 1. When? 2. Where? 3.
