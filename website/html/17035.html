{{Orphan|date=February 2009}} {{Infobox Company | name = Hudson Valley Federal Credit Union | company_logo = [[File:HVFCU logo.jpg]] | type = [[Credit union]] | foundation = IBM Poughkeepsie Employees Federal Credit Union, [[Poughkeepsie (city), New York|Poughkeepsie, New York]], (1963) | location = [[Poughkeepsie (city), New York|Poughkeepsie, New York]], [[United States]] | key_people = Mary Madden, President & CEO | industry = [[Financial Services]] | products = | assets = $ | num_employees = 776 (as of 9/28/10) | homepage = [https://www.hvfcu.org/ hvfcu.org] }} '''Hudson Valley Federal Credit Union''' (often shortened to '''HVFCU''') is a [[credit union]] in the [[New York]] counties of [[Dutchess County|Dutchess]], [[Orange County, New York|Orange]], [[Ulster County|Ulster]] and [[Putnam County|Putnam]]. {{As of|9/10}}, the institution had 220,000+ members and approximately 776 employees. It is one of the largest credit unions in the United States. HVFCU offers a wide variety of services, including [[checking accounts]], [[savings accounts]], vehicle loans, home equity loans and lines of credit, mortgages, financial planning through HVFCU Financial Services, property & casualty insurance through Insurance Agency of the Hudson Valley, business loans and accounts, and [[VISA]] debit, credit, and gift cards. HVFCU offers its members access to over 72,000 surcharge-free ATMs nationwide in the Allpoint, CO-OP, Presto!, and SUM ATM Networks. In 2010, it was named one of the Best Companies to Work For in New York. ==History== In October 1963, a group of IBM employees had a visionâ€”to create a financial cooperative for IBM employees and their families in Poughkeepsie and Fishkill, New York. Their product line in the beginning was modest, consisting of basic savings and consumer loans. By the end of their first year, the credit union had 14 member accounts and $172 in assets. The name of the credit union changed a few times over the years, and in 1994, the name changed to the present-day name of Hudson Valley Federal Credit Union. At the beginning of 2003, membership requirements were changed to allow those who live, work, worship, volunteer, or attend school in Dutchess, Orange, or Ulster counties to join the credit union. {{cite web|url=https://www.hvfcu.org/about-us/membership |title=Hudson Valley Federal Credit Union Membership |accessdate=2010-04-12 }} On July, 1, 2010, HVFCU merged with Putnam Federal Credit Union, in Putnam County, New York. As a result of the merger, individuals who live, work, worship, volunteer, or attend school in Putnam County, are also eligible to become members of HVFCU. == Branches == HVFCU has branches in the following locations: Arlington, Beacon, Brewster, Carmel, Fishkill, Hopewell Junction, Highland, Hyde Park, Kingston, Middletown, New Windsor, Newburgh, Pleasant Valley, Poughkeepsie (159 Barnegat Rd), Poughkeepsie (1 Civic Center Plaza) and Wappingers Falls (Hollowbrook). {{cite web|url=https://www.hvfcu.org/about-us/branches-atms#branch-locations |title=Hudson Valley Federal Credit Union Branches & ATMs |accessdate=2010-08-30 }} The credit union also has four branch in IBM buildings in Poughkeepsie and East Fishkill.
