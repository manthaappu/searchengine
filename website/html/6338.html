{{Cleanup|date=December 2010}} The '''Civil Pages''' is a computer system that acts as a private social media site for the UK [[Civil Service]]. It was set up by [[The National Archives]] on behalf of the [[Cabinet Office]] http://www.official-documents.gov.uk/document/hc0809/hc04/0469/0469.pdf.It debuted shortly after an incident where the head of [[MI6]] was identified in a holiday snap on facebook, and was somewhat controversial due the state of government finances at the time. The head of the Civil Service, [[Gus O'Donnell]] described civil pages as '...the Facebook of the Civil Service... without the man in the Speedos'.
