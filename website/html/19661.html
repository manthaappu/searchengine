[[File:Davenport.JPG|thumb|right|James Davenport in 2001]] '''James Harold Davenport''' (born 1953) is a British [[computer scientist]] who works in [[computer algebra]]. He is the Hebron and Medlock Professor of Information Technology at the [[University of Bath]] in [[Bath, Somerset|Bath]], [[England]]. He is an author of a textbook about computer algebra and many papers. He has been Project Chair of the European OpenMath Project and its successor [[Thematic Network]], with responsibilities for aligning [[OpenMath]] and [[MathML]], producing Content Dictionaries and supervised a Reduce-based OpenMath/MathML translator, and was Treasurer of the [[European Mathematical Trust]].
