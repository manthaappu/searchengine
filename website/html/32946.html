'''SPOJ''' (Sphere Online Judge) is an [[online judge]] system with over 75,000 registered users and over 6000 problems. The solution to problems can be submitted in over 40 languages including [[C (programming language)|C]], [[C++]], [[Python (programming language)|Python]], [[C Sharp (programming language)|C#]], [[Go (programming language)|Go]], [[Haskell (programming language)|Haskell]], [[Ocaml]], [[F Sharp (programming language)|F#]]. SPOJ has a rapidly growing problem set/tasks available for practice 24 hours/day, including many original tasks prepared by the community of expert problem setters associated with the project.
