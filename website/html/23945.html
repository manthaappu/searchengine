There are a number of '''medical royal colleges''' in the [[United Kingdom]]. Each is responsible for a different speciality within the medical field. They are general charged with supervising the training of doctors within that speciality, although that responsibility has been somewhat taken over by the [[Postgraduate Medical Education and Training Board]] in recent years.
