{{Orphan|date=September 2008}} The '''Brandon-Hill list''' is a list of [[Academic journal|journals]] and [[book]]s recommended for a small [[hospital]] library. Compiled by Alfred Brandon and Dorothy Hill,{{cite web |url=http://www.mssm.edu/library/brandon-hill/ |title=Mount Sinai - Brandon Hill Selected Lists - Levy Library |format= |work= |accessdate=2008-09-15 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080608215751/http://www.mssm.edu/library/brandon-hill/ |archivedate = 2008-06-08}} the 2003 version included 434 books and subscriptions to 79 journals,{{cite journal |author=Hill DR, Stickell HN |title=Brandon/Hill selected list of print books and journals in allied health |journal=J Med Libr Assoc |volume=91 |issue=1 |pages=18–33 |year=2003 |month=January |pmid=12568155 |pmc=141184 |doi= |url=}} but is no longer updated.{{cite journal |author=Shedlock J, Walton LJ |title=Developing a virtual community for health sciences library book selection: Doody's Core Titles |journal=J Med Libr Assoc |volume=94 |issue=1 |pages=61–6 |year=2006 |month=January |pmid=16404471 |pmc=1324773 |doi= |url=}} It has been used to calculate the rate of rise of journal costs.{{cite journal |author=Schlimgen JB, Kronenfeld MR |title=Update on inflation of journal prices: Brandon/Hill list journals and the scientific, technical, and medical publishing market |journal=J Med Libr Assoc |volume=92 |issue=3 |pages=307–14 |year=2004 |month=July |pmid=15243636 |pmc=442172 |doi= |url=}} ==References== {{reflist}} {{medicine-stub}} [[Category:Medical literature]]
