'''State insects''' are designated by 41 individual states of the fifty [[United States]]. Some states have more than one designated insect, or have multiple categories (e.g., state insect and state butterfly, etc.). More than half of the insects chosen are not native to [[North America]], because of the inclusion of three [[Europe]]an species ([[European honey bee]], [[European mantis]], and [[Coccinella septempunctata|7-spotted ladybug]]). {| class="wikitable" |- bgcolor="#CCCCCC" !
