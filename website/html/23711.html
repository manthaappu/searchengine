'''Mathematical puzzles''' make up an integral part of [[recreational mathematics]]. They have specific rules as do [[multiplayer game]]s, but they do not usually involve competition between two or more players. Instead, to solve such a [[puzzle]], the solver must find a solution that satisfies the given conditions. Mathematical puzzles require mathematics to solve them. [[Logic puzzle]]s are a common type of mathematical puzzle. [[Conway's Game of Life]] and [[fractals]], as two examples, may also be considered mathematical puzzles even though the solver interacts with them only at the beginning by providing a set of initial conditions.
