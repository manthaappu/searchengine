{{Orphan|date=September 2010}} '''Daniel Gajski''' is a Professor of the School of Information and Computer Science and the School of Engineering at [[University of California, Irvine]], [[USA]].[http://www.faculty.uci.edu/profile.cfm?faculty_id=2370&name=Daniel%20D.%20Gajski UCI Faculty Profile for Dr. Daniel Gajski], retrieved 2010-04-07. He is also the Director for the Center for Embedded Computer Systems (CECS).[http://www.cecs.uci.edu/mem_staff.htm Center for Embedded Computer Systems Staff Page], retrieved 2010-04-07. After 10 years of industrial experience in Europe and the United States in digital circuits, telecommunication systems, supercomputer design, and VLSI structures, he spent 10 years in academia with the Department of Computer Science at the [[University of Illinois]] at Urbana-Champaign. His research interests include [[embedded systems]] and [[information technology]], design methodologies, specification languages and [[CAD software]], and the science of design.[http://www.cecs.uci.edu/~gajski/ Daniel Gajski's UCI Web Page], retrieved 2010-04-07. In 1994, Daniel Gajski became an IEEE fellow for his work in VLSD, CAD tools, and system level design methodology.[http://www.ieee.org/membership_services/membership/fellows/alphabetical/gfellows.html#Ga IEEE - Fellows - G], [[IEEE]]. Accessed April 27, 2010. Election citation: ''for contributions to VLSI and system level design methodologies and CAD tools''. On January 10, 2010, the European Design and Automation Association (EDAA) announced that the EDAA Lifetime Achievement Award goes to Dr. Daniel Gajski.[http://www.edaa.com/press_releases/EDAA_Press_Release_2010gajski.pdf EDAA January 2010 Press Release], retrieved 2001-04-07 This award is given to individuals who have made outstanding contributions to the state of the art in electronic design, automation and testing of electronic systems in their life. ==Selected Books== * D.
