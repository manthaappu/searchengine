[[File:Business-Motivation-Model-top.gif|right|500px|thumb|Business Motivation Model.]] The '''Business Motivation Model''' (BMM) in [[enterprise architecture]] provides a scheme and structure for developing, communicating, and managing [[business plan]]s in an organized manner. Specifically, the Business Motivation Model does all of the following: * identifies factors that motivate the establishing of business plans; * identifies and defines the elements of business plans; and * indicates how all these factors and elements inter-relate. Among these elements are those that provide governance for and guidance to the business — Business Policies and Business Rules.
