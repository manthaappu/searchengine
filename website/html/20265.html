Offices in [[New York, NY]], [[United States|USA]] and [[Bangalore]], [[India]] | industry = [[Mobile media]], [[Mobile Internet]], [[Mobile 2.0]] | products = Mi Platform - Publishing & Distribution, Personalization & User Management, Ad Management | homepage = [http://www.julysystems.com/ www.julysystems.com] }} '''July Systems,'''a mobile media company, was co-founded in 2001 {{cite journal | title =July Systems, Inc. | journal =Business Week | url =http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=1997546 }} by the technology entrepreneur Rajesh TS Reddy {{cite journal | title =There's Something About Rajesh | journal =Forbes Magazine, July 2009 | url =http://business.in.com/article/work-in-progress/theres-something-about-rajesh/2372/1 }} . The company's primary business is powering mobile internet channels - mobile web and applications - for media brands. July enables publishing, distribution, user management & personalization, and ad inventory monetization on mobile. Its customers are primarily in the United States and India markets, and include several well known US and India media houses. July Systems is headquartered in Los Angeles with offices in New York City, and Bangalore, India. == Products== July Systems’ Mi - '' Mobile Internet''- Platform helps brands publish and distribute on the mobile internet, monetize inventory, and personalize services for consumers. A Mobile 2.0 publishing platform for media, entertainment, news, and sports brands – it enables them to distribute simultaneously to the mobile web, and mobile app stores. These user-centric [[mobile internet]] channels are said to result in increased usage, advertising inventory for publishers, and an engaged opportunity for advertisers. For consumers,the Mi Platform is said to provide 'live, personalized,interactive and multi-media' experiences. July Systems' mobile internet offerings comprise: '''Publishing & Distribution''' to produce and distribute content on the mobile internet - mobile web, application stores, carrier decks. The July Systems publishing platform comprises editorial and management tools that enable media brands to produce and manage content across their mobile properties.
'''Personalization & User Management''' to engage with consumers and build a user base. '''Mi box''' is a personalized notification service for users on mobile internet services over multiple delivery channels. This improves and increases consumer engagement with the mobile portal, making it sticky for repeat user visits.
'''Ad Management''' that is centralized with consolidated reporting across ad providers and distribution channels. It includes a monetization program for mobile portals that comprises the technology platform, management tools and business relationships required to increase revenues from mobile inventory. A partner program also enables advertisers to use advertising inventory across July powered mobile properties. Per the company, the Mi Platform enables brands to establish a long-term relationship with their consumer on the [[mobile web]]. Typical customers that use July's mobile platform and services are media companies in news, entertainment, news and business, besides [[mobile advertising]] companies, and ad networks. '''Mobile 2.0'''
[[Mobile 2.0]] is the next generation of the mobile internet. In addition to Web 2.0 attributes like social networking and interactivity it includes mobile centric characteristics like personal and real-time notifications, instant phone-to-phone sharing and always-on.
