{{Infobox OS | name = Prex | logo = | screenshot = | caption = | developer = | source_model = [[Open source]] | kernel_type = [[Microkernel]] | supported_platforms = [[x86 architecture|x86]], [[ARM architecture|ARM]], [[PowerPC]] | ui = | family = [[Real-time operating system]]s | released = | latest_release_version = | latest_release_date = | latest_test_version = 0.9.0 | latest_test_date = 1 Oct 2009 | marketing_target = | programmed_in = [[C (programming language)|C]] | prog_language = | language = | updatemodel = | package_manager = | working_state = | license = [[BSD license]] | website = [http://prex.sourceforge.net/ Prex] }} '''Prex''' is a [[Porting|portable]] [[32-bit]] [[embedded operating system|embedded]] [[real-time operating system|real-time]] [[microkernel]] [[operating system]]. The [[Kernel (computer science)|kernel]] is compact, under 25k [[byte]]s, and provides [[memory management]], [[inter-process communication]] (messages and [[shared memory]]) and [[Thread (computer science)|multithreading]] in the kernel, and leaves more [[Application layer|application]]-specific features like a [[file system]] to be implemented. It is designed to have virtual [[Computer storage|memory]] and memory protection when available. It is currently pre-[[Software release life cycle|beta]], but runs on [[x86 architecture|x86]] PCs and the [[Game Boy Advance]]. It has an [[open source]] [[BSD license]]. It is programmed in the [[C (programming language)|C]] [[programming language]], and has a [[POSIX]] [[Emulator|emulation]] layer. Version 0.9.0 was released on 1 Oct 2009.
