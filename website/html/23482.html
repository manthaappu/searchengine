The NYSC is funded by the [http://www.nysf.com/ NYSF] Mayer received her B.S. in [[Symbolic system]]s, graduating [[with honors]], and M.S. in [[Computer Science]] from [[Stanford University]]. For both degrees, she specialized in [[artificial intelligence]]. In 2009, the [[Illinois Institute of Technology]] granted Mayer an [[honoris causa]] doctorate degree honoring her pathfinding work in the field of search.{{cite news| title = 50.
