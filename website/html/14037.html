The '''Foundational Model of Anatomy Ontology''' or the '''FMA''', is a reference [[Ontology (computer science)|ontology]] for the domain of [[anatomy]]. It is a symbolic representation of the canonical, phenotypic structure of an [[organism]]; a spatial-structural ontology of anatomical entities and relations which form the physical organization of an organism at all salient levels of [[granularity]]. ==See also== * [[Ontology (computer science)]] ==External links== *[http://sig.biostr.washington.edu/projects/fm/AboutFM.html The Foundational Model of Anatomy Ontology] {{Semantic Web}} {{Bioinformatics-stub}} [[Category:Bioinformatics]] [[Category:Ontology (information science)]] [[de:Foundational Model of Anatomy]] [[zh:解剖学基础模型]]
