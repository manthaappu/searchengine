{{unsourced|date=September 2010}} {{orphan|date=December 2008}} The absolute debugger is a legacy UNIX debugger found on Solaris 1 and 2, HP-UX and SCO. A version of ADB was integrated into the [[BSD]] kernel as a kernel debugger. Today, the kernel debugger in *BSD is the only derivative of ADB still in regular use. Use of ADB was largely replaced by the symbolic debugger [[dbx debugger|DBX]]. [[Category:Debuggers]] {{programming-software-stub}}
