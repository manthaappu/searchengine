{{Orphan|date=February 2009}} {{Infobox Software | name = CitySurf Globe | logo = [[Image:Citysurflogo.jpg]] | screenshot = [[Image:Citysurfglobe.jpg]] | caption = | developer = [[PiriReis Bilişim Teknolojileri]] | released = | latest_release_version = | latest_release_date = | programming language = | size = | operating system = [[Windows 2000]], [[Windows XP]], [[Windows Server 2003]]| | language = English, Turkish, Arabic | genre = [[GIS]] | license = | website = http://www.citysurf.com.tr }} '''CitySurf Globe''' is a family of Geography 2.0 [[Geographical Information System]] software, developed by [[PiriReis Bilişim Teknolojileri]], known as a new model in mapping servers and interaction with the final user. '''CitySurf Globe''' family models raster data, such as high resolution [[satellite images]] and aerial images, under high speeds using [[digital terrain model]] and vector [[geographical information systems]] and accordingly serves these via internet or local nets. Prepares geographical analyses and arranges the geographical data thanks to its interaction with the final user. '''CitySurf Globe''' has an [[OpenGL]]-based viewer, which connects to the servers for streaming of content, elevation, satellite and/or airphoto imagery, roads, 3D buildings, points of interest, etc. This is similar to [[Google Earth]], [[Microsoft Virtual Earth]], and [[ArcGIS]] concepts. ==CitySurf Globe Screenshots== Image:Citysurfturism.JPG| Image:Citysurf3d.JPG| Image:Citysurfwikipedi.JPG| == Applications Areas == * 3D city information system * 3D visualization of for civil engineering, landscape design and urban planning * Military and defence * Real estate industry * Oil, gas and electric industry * Travel and tourism [[Category:Earth sciences graphics software]]
