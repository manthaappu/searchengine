{{Unreferenced|date=September 2009}} {{Expert-subject|Science|date=February 2009}} '''Representation''', from the most general and abstract [[systemics|systemic]] perspective, relates to a [[role]] or [[Function (engineering)|function]] or a property of an [[abstraction|abstract]] or real [[object]], relation or [[change]]s. For example, * an [[ambassador]] or a sport team may represent its nation. * graphical figures or written symbolic text may represent some abstract ideas or physical objects. In the case of humans or human-made objects, a representation can be: * formal or informal, i.e. "legal" or really realized with a consensus of interested community. * on the behalf of (or approved by) represented subject or without his/her/their permission. ==See also== *[[simulation]] *[[imitation]] *[[Social responsibility|responsibility]] *[[delegation]] *[[metaphor]] ==References== {{reflist}} [[Category:Metaphors]] {{sci-stub}}
