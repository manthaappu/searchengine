(2009) | }} '''Insects''' is a 2009 album released by [[Gibraltarian people|Gibraltarian]] [[flamenco]]/[[metal music|metal]] quintet [[Breed 77]]. It was released on 16 November 2009{{cite web |url=http://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B002SZQYY6 |title=Amazon UK album page }} on LaRocka Records and contains 12 songs, including 2 bonus tracks. Breed 77 headlined a United Kingdom tour in support of the album in November 2009, titled "''The Insects Are Here''" tour. Support acts were Symphony Cult and Our Malevolent Tyranny. Cars On Fire also supported the band on some of the tour dates as well. In December 2009 the band are also touring in support of the album in Spain. "The Battle of Hatin" was available as a free download from their website prior to the release of ''Insects''. ''Wake Up'' was the first single released from the album, and a music video, directed by Mario Ruiz has been produced.{{cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zijMHn5q6fA |title=''Wake Up'' |publisher=[[YouTube]]}} [[Zombie (song)#Cover versions|Zombie]] is to be released as a single around March 2010, and a music video has been produced.{{cite web |url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XJFmh6W1Jjo |title=''Zombie'' |publisher=YouTube}} The album was given an international re-release in May and June 2010, with [[Breed 77]] signing a worldwide deal with earMusic / Edel GmbH. The re-released version of the album comes packaged in a [[digipak]], with an extra bonus track 'Save Us', which also featured on the 'Wake Up' single.
