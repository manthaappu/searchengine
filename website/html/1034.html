{{Unreferenced|date=August 2008}} '''Adjustment handles''' are the small boxes appearing on the corners and edges of a "selected control" that let you change the size and shape of the control. By [[dragging and dropping]] the control handle on an edge, you can make the control wider or narrower, taller or shorter. Corner handles let you move two edges at once.
