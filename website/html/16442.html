In [[computer security]], '''heap feng shui''' is a technique used in [[exploit (computer security)|exploits]] to facilitate [[arbitrary code execution]].{{cite web|url=http://blog.trendmicro.com/heaps-and-bounds/|title=Heaps and Bounds|publisher=Trend Micro|date=September 3rd, 2007|accessdate=2009-08-10}} The technique attempts to manipulate the layout of the [[Heap (programming)|heap]] by making heap allocations of carefully selected sizes.
