{{DISPLAYTITLE:''Fauna Entomologica Scandinavica''}} '''''Fauna Entomologica Scandinavica''''' is a scientific book series of [[entomology|entomological]] identification manuals for [[insect]]s (and other terrestrial [[arthropod]]s) in North-West Europe, mainly Fennoscandia and Denmark. The series is used by a number of groups, such as ecologists, biologists, and insect collectors.
