It has about 1500 male and female pupils, aged 11–18. It is a specialist [[Maths and Computing College]]. The sixth form was deemed extremely good in the last Ofsted inspection. A wide array of [[GCSE]] options is available to pupils in year nine; college placements are also available. [[File:Lawnswood School, East Building, Leeds - geograph.org.uk - 98593.jpg|thumb|left|The previous buildings, vacated by the school in 2003.]] It was founded in 1972 and had its first comprehensive intake in 1974.
