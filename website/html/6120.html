The player uses an iconic programming language to teach a virtual robot how to navigate various mazes. The gameplay straddled the line between entertainment and programming education. ChipWits won numerous awards, including MACazine Best of '85, and MacUser's Editor's Choice 1985 Award. The game was developed in [[MacFORTH]], and later ported to the [[Apple II]] and [[Commodore 64]].
