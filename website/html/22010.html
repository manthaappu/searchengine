{{In-universe/Comics|date=October 2009}} {{Inappropriate tone|date=December 2007}} '''User Friendly characters''' are the characters that feature on the [[webcomic]] [[User Friendly]]. ==Main characters== ===A.J.=== :''First Appearance: [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=19971117&mode=classic November 17, 1997]'' '''A.J.''' Garrett is the creative guy for the company, maintaining and designing their websites. As a [[web designer]], he's uncomfortably crammed in that tiny crevice between the techies and the [[marketing]] people. This means he's not disliked by anyone, but they all look at him funny from time to time. A.J. is shy and sensitive. He loves most computer games, nifty art, and has a big brother relationship with the [[#Dust Puppy|Dust Puppy]]. A.J. is terrified of [[Grue (monster)|Grues]] and attempts to avoid [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=20021017 them]. A.J. was downsized on [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=19980905&mode=classic September 5, 1998], following iCan't Internet's purchase of Columbia Internet on [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=19980824&mode=classic August 24, 1998], but was re-engaged on [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=19981017&mode=classic October 17, 1998] after the company had been rebought. He lost his job again on [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=20021016 October 16, 2002], following working on a personal project, and was employed by [[AOL]]. Having suffered an [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=20021028 Ethicectomy], he became a clone of Stef - fortunately following an Ethics donation from [[Colin Powell]] he was [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=20021129&mode=classic re-employed]. He and Miranda have previously dated, but split up over a misunderstanding. They remained attracted to each other and started dating again on September 16, 2005. * [http://www.userfriendly.org/cartoons/aj/ A.J.'s cast description] * [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=19990401&mode=classic A.J. climbs to new heights] ===The Chief=== :''First Appearance: [http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=19971119&mode=classic November 19, 1997]'' The '''Chief''' is Columbia Internet's [[CEO]]. He is the fearless leader for the techies and salespeople, as well as the only calm, clear-thinking head in the company. He wouldn't be if he knew what was really going on. The Chief is also a closet [[guitar]] player, having spent years mastering the art of perfect tone, only to have [[computers]] come along and render all of his effort meaningless. Illiad based the character on a former boss: {{cquote|The Chief is based on my business mentor. He was the vice president that I reported to back in the day. The Chief, like my mentor, is tall (!) and thin and sports a bushy ring around a bald crown, plus a very thick moustache.}} The Chief bears a superficial resemblance to the [[Pointy-Haired Boss]] of [[Dilbert]] fame — he once was referred as the “Pointy Headed Boss”, and asked whether it was a mistake[http://ars.userfriendly.org/cartoons/?id=20000903]. However, Illiad says that The Chief was not inspired by the Dilbert character.
