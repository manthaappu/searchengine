{{Advert|date=September 2010}} '''eLearnSecurity Certified Professional Penetration Tester''' (eCPPT) is an [[Ethical Hacking]] and [[Penetration Testing]] [[Professional certification]] offered by [[eLearnSecurity]]
eCPPT exam requires the student to carry out a real penetration test against a target and to build a penetration testing report.
The eCPPT is currently the only security certification requiring the pentest of real targets and the production of a [[penetration testing]] report. ==Penetration Testing Course - Professional== The eCPPT exam can be taken within 120 days from the date of enrollment in the Penetration Testing Course - Professional.
Penetration Testing Course is the distance learning course delivered in [[e-learning]] format dubbed as "What [[Certified Ethical Hacker|CEH]] should have been" http://www.ethicalhacker.net/content/view/307/24/http://www.darknet.org.uk/2010/05/elearnsecurity-online-penetration-testing-training/http://www.securityaegis.com/review-elearnsecuritys-penetration-testing-pro.
The course is divided into three main knowledge domains covered by [[Flash animation|flash]] slides and video traininghttp://www.elearnsecurity.com/course/penetration_testing/contents.php:
# [[Web Application Security]] # [[Network Security]] # [[System Security]]
