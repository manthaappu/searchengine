The '''Paedophile Unit''' is a branch of the [[United Kingdom|British]] [[London]] [[Metropolitan Police Service]]'s [[Child Abuse Investigation Team|Child Abuse Investigation Command]], based at [[Scotland Yard]]. It deals with organised crime and the manufacture and distribution of [[child pornography]].[http://www.met.police.uk/scd/specialist_units/child_abuse.htm Child Abuse Investigation Command] The Unit was the subject of a series of [[BBC]] [[television]] programmes concerning the "Hunt for Britainâ€™s Paedophiles" which were the subject of a record 23,000 calls to the BBC Audience Line.[http://www.bbc.co.uk/pressoffice/pressreleases/stories/2002/06_june/19/paedophiles.shtml Inside the Mind of a Paedophile] They were responsible for the break-up of the pedophile advocacy group called the [[Paedophile Information Exchange]].[http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/england/leicestershire/6944369.stm Paedophile campaigner is jailed] The Paedophile Unit works in conjunction with the Hi-Tech Crime Unit to examine computers used by suspected offenders, including computers used in public areas or via remote connections.
