'''Orion''' is a [[system-on-a-chip]] manufactured by [[Marvell Technology Group]] and used in [[network-attached storage]]. Based on the [[ARM architecture]], it has on-chip support for [[Ethernet]], [[Serial ATA|SATA]] and [[Universal Serial Bus|USB]], and is used in hardware made by [[Hewlett-Packard]] and [[D-Link]] among others.[http://www.cyrius.com/debian/orion/ Debian on Marvell Orion] It is supported by the Lenny release of [[Debian|Debian GNU/Linux]].[http://www.debian.org/News/2009/20090214 Debian GNU/Linux 5.0 released] ==References== {{reflist}} {{Compu-hardware-stub}} [[Category:ARM architecture]] [[Category:Electronic design]] [[Category:Microtechnology]] [[Category:Network-attached storage]]
