{{Unreferenced|date=July 2009}} '''Provider Edge router''' ('''PE router''') is a [[router]] between one [[computer network|network]] service provider's area and areas administered by other network providers. A network provider is usually an [[Internet Service Provider]] as well (or only that). The term ''PE router'' covers equipment capable of a broad range of [[routing]] [[Communications protocol|protocol]]s, notably: *[[Border Gateway Protocol]] (BGP) *[[Open Shortest Path First]] (OSPF) *[[Multi-protocol label switching|Multi-Protocol Label Switching]] (MPLS) PE routers need not be aware of what kind of traffic is coming from the provider's network. However, some PE routers also do labeling. ==See also== * [[CE router]] {{DEFAULTSORT:Pe Router}} [[Category:Routers]] [[Category:MPLS networking]]
