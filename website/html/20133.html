{{orphan|date=August 2010}} {{Notability|Biographies|date=October 2009}} {{BLP sources|date=October 2009}} '''Jonathan Baginski''', born August 9, 1985 in the city of Brisbane, Australia, is an award-winning photographer, video game artist and musician. ==Biography== Jonathan was born in Sydney, Australia and raised in Brisbane. He attended College studying B/W photography and Multimedia. In 2008 Jonathan had left his day job as working in the games industry, to fulfill his desire to learn more about photography and film making. In 2009, Australian rock band, [[Wolfmother]] appointed Jonathan as their Tour and Photo Journal Photographer. His works are present throughout numerous publications such as [[Rolling Stone]] and online media and he continues to work with and learn from some of the best in the industry.
