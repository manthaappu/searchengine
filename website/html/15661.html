'''Grammatical Framework''' (GF) is a type-theoretic grammar formalism based on [[Martin-Löf type theory]]. GF particularly addresses four aspects of grammars: multilinguality, semantics, modularity and grammar engineering, and reuse of grammars in different formats and as software components. ==References== * {{Citation | last = Ranta | first = Aarne | title = Grammatical Framework: A Type-Theoretical Grammar Formalism | journal = Journal of Functional Programming | volume = 14 | issue = 2 | pages = 145–189 | year = 2004 | url = http://www.cs.chalmers.se/~aarne/articles/gf-jfp.ps.gz }} ==External links== * [http://gf.digitalgrammars.com/ Grammatical Framework homepage].
