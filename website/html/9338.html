In [[telecommunication]]s, a '''data bank''' is a repository of information on one or more subjects that is organized in a way that facilitates local or remote information retrieval. A data bank may be either centralized or decentralized. In this sense, data bank is [[synonym]]ous with [[database]]. '''Data bank''' may also refer to an organization primarily concerned with the construction and maintenance of a [[database]]. ==Sources== *{{FS1037C MS188}} *''[[The American Heritage Dictionary of the English Language]], Fourth Edition''.
