Its founder is [[Russian government]]al agency [[FAPMC]] which oversees country's [[mass media]]. First awarded in 2004, the prize honors top [[Russian language]] (mainly Russia-based) [[website]]s in several categories. In 2006, 2007 and 2009 a Runet Prize in the category "Science and education" was awarded to the [[Russian Wikipedia|Russian language version of
