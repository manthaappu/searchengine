{{Unreferenced|date=December 2009}} {{For|the North East Wales Institute of Higher Education ("NEWI")|North East Wales Institute of Higher Education}} '''Newi''' is an [[acronym]] for '''NEw World Infrastructure''', a [[software architecture]] for [[software componentry]], mostly known as '''Newi Business Objects''' which coined the term ''[[Business object (computer science)|business object]]''. Newi was developed by [[Integrated Object Systems]], [[England]]. It was one of the first implemented architectures for software components.
