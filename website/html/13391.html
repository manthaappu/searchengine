The '''Federated Computing Research Conference''', '''FCRC''', is an event that brings together several [[academic conference]]s, workshops, and plenary talks in the field of [[computer science]]. FCRC has been organised in 1993, 1996, 1999, 2003, and 2007; the next event will be held in 2011. In the first FCRC, the main organiser was the [[Computing Research Association]]; since then, the [[Association for Computing Machinery]] has taken the lead in organising the event.
