The '''CESG Listed Advisor Scheme''' is a programme run by [[Government Communications Headquarters#CESG|CESG]], to provide a pool of [[information assurance]] consultants to government departments and other public-sector bodies in the [[United Kingdom|UK]].{{cite web|url=http://www.cesg.gov.uk/products_services/iacs/clas/index.shtml|accessdate=2010-11-02|title=CESG Listed Adviser Scheme (CLAS) - What is CLAS?}} CLAS consultants advise on systems that handle [[Classified information in the United Kingdom#System of classification|protectively marked information]], up to and including secret;{{cite web|url=http://www.vega.co.uk/services/information_assurance/penetration_testing_services/clas.aspx|accessdate=2010-11-02|title=CLAS - CESG Listed Adviser Scheme - VEGA Consulting Services|quote=protect}} for instance, they may advise on [[Government Secure Intranet|GSI]] accreditation.{{cite web|url=http://www.nta-monitor.com/services/consultancyservices.html|accessdate=2010-11-02|title=NTA Monitor - Services - consultancy|date=2010-10-14}} CLAS consultants hold a security clearance, at least [[Classified information in the United Kingdom#Security Check (SC)|SC]].{{cite web|url=http://www.sapphire.net/clas-consultancy-7/clas-consultancy-68.html|accessdate=2010-11-02|title=CLAS Consultancy}} CLAS consultants may develop risk assessments in line with [[HMG Infosec Standard No.1|IS1]], and Risk Management and Accreditation Document Set(s) (RMADS) in line with [[HMG Infosec Standard No.2|IS2]].{{cite web|url=http://www.commissum.com/consulting/clas/|accessdate=2010-11-02|title=CESG Listed Advisor Scheme | Commissum|date=2010-11-02}} ==External Links== * [http://www.cesg.gov.uk/products_services/iacs/clas/index.shtml CLAS homepage] * [https://www.clasconsultants.net/kb/index.php/Main_Page CLAS knowledgebase] ==References== {{reflist}} [[Category:Information assurance standards]] [[Category:Information Risk Management]]
