{{Multiple issues|wikify = January 2011|dead end = January 2011|orphan = September 2010|notability = December 2010}} '''Webcare''' is defined as the act of managing online interactions between a company and a customer. ==Goal of webcare== The goal of webcare is to improve relationship quality with the customer or start a new relationship. Companies may employ a dedicated webcare team to respond to comments and or complaints online. ==Alternate definition of webcare== By Andy Hoek[http://andyhoek.posterous.com/post/1115160517] ==References== [[Category:Internet]]
