The '''Cambridge Computer Lab Ring''' is a not-for-profit independent members' association that campaigns on behalf of Cambridge computer scientists to build the Cambridge community in computing. It was launched in October 2002 to provide graduates of the [[Cambridge University Computer Laboratory]] with a lifetime benefit from their Cambridge degrees.
