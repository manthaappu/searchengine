{{orphan|date=September 2010}} {{notability|date=December 2010}} {{Expert-subject|computing|article|date=May 2008}} [[File:A Black and White rectangular Monochrome Bitmap.PNG|framed|A basic monochrome bitmap]] A '''Monochrome bitmap''' is black and white bitmap file. A monochrome bitmap is a rectangular grid of pixels, but each pixel is represented by a single bit. Eight pixels are in each byte of the bitmap. Monochrome bitmaps are often used for bitmapped text or single-colored images.[http://fastgraph.com/help/monochrome_bitmaps.html fastgraph.com/help/monochrome_bitmaps.html] == References == {{Reflist}} {{Compu-stub}} [[Category:Computer graphics]]
