{{citations missing|date= September 2008}} '''ShareOffice''' is an [[online office suite]] built on top of the open standards known as OpenSAM (short for "open simple [[Ajax (programming)|AJAX]] [[Mashup (web application hybrid)|mashup]]"). Launched in May 2007 by [[ShareMethods]]{{cite web|url=http://itredux.com/2007/07/26/opensam-is-promising/ |title=OpenSAM is Promising |author=Ismael Ghalimi |publisher=ITRedux |date=2007-07-26 |accessdate=2007-07-26 }}, the application incorporates, through partnership, a word processor from [[iNetOffice]], a spreadsheet from [[EditGrid]], and a presentation application from [[Preezo]].
