[[Maryland]] is among the states in the US that is acquiring [[LIDAR]] data, complementing the [[National LIDAR Dataset - USA]] effort to acquire high resolution elevation data across the nation. The Maryland [http://www.dnr.state.md.us/ Dept. of Natural Resources] coordinates with most counties in the state regarding acquisition specifications, post-processing quality control, and in some cases, distribution.
