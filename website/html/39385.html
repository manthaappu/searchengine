'''Victor R. Basili''', born April 13, 1940 in Brooklyn, New York,{{cite web | title = Curriculum vitae | date = 2008-11-14 | url = http://www.cs.umd.edu/~basili/CV%20basili.pdf | format = PDF | accessdate = 2008-11-18 }} is an Emeritus Professor at the Department of Computer Science and the Institute for Advanced Computer Studies, both at [[University of Maryland, College Park|University of Maryland]]. He holds a [[Ph.D.]] in Computer Science from the [[University of Texas at Austin]] and two honorary degrees.{{cite web | last = Computer Aid, Inc. | title = Focus on Dr. Victor Basili: A CAI State of the Practice Interview | date = [[01-2007]] | url = http://www.compaid.com/caiinternet/ezine/vicbasiliinterview.pdf | format = PDF | accessdate = 2008-11-12 }} He is currently a Senior Research Fellow at the [http://fc-md.umd.edu/fcmd/index.html Fraunhofer Center for Experimental Software Engineering - Maryland] and from 1997-2004 was its Executive Director.{{cite web | title = Dr. Victor R. Basili UMD home page | url = http://www.cs.umd.edu/~basili/ | accessdate = 2008-11-12 }} He is well-known for his works on measuring, evaluating, and improving the [[Software development process]], especially his papers on the [[GQM|Goal/Question /Metric Approach]], the [[Quality Improvement Paradigm]], and the [[Experience Factory]].{{cite web | title = Victor R.
