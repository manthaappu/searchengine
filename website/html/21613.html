'''Lieuwe Dirk Boonstra''' (born 1905, date of death unknown) was a [[South Africa]]n [[paleontologist]] whose work focused on the [[therapsida|mammal-like reptiles]] of the Middle ([[Tapinocephalus Assemblage Zone|''Tapinocephalus'' Assemblage Zone]]) and Late [[Permian]], whose fossil remains are common in the South African [[Karroo]].
