{{notability|date=January 2011}} {{primarysources|date=January 2011}} The '''Berkeley Lower Extremity Exoskeleton''' ('''BLEEX''') is a [[robot]]ic device that attaches to the lower [[body]]. Its [[purpose]] is to complement the pilot's [[Physical strength|strength]] by adding extra [[force]] to one's lower extremity bodily movements. The BLEEX was funded by the [[Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency]] (DARPA), and [[Technology development|developed]] by the Berkeley Robotics and Human Engineering Laboratory, a unit within the [[University of California, Berkeley]] [[UC Berkeley College of Engineering|Department of Mechanical Engineering]].
