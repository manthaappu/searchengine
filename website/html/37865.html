'''Thomas Alan Berson''' is a cryptographer and [[computer security]] researcher. His notable work includes several [[cryptanalysis|cryptanalytic attacks]], and research in the practical use of [[cryptographic protocol]]s, particularly in computer networks. A founding member of the [[International Association for Cryptologic Research]], Tom Berson has been one of its officers since 1983, and was the first person selected as an IACR Fellow.[http://www.iacr.org/fellows/2004/berson.html IACR Fellow citation] He served as editor of the ''[[Journal of Cryptology]]'' from 1986 to 2001.
