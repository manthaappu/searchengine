'''Mordechai (Moti) Ben-Ari ''' ({{lang-he|'''מרדכי (מוטי) בן-ארי'''}}) is a professor of [[computer science]] education at the [[Weizmann Institute of Science]]. Ben-Ari has published numerous textbooks in computer science, developed software tools for teaching CS, and written many influential papers in CS education. His primary focus has been on books and tools for learning theoretical concepts in computer science, such as [[Concurrent_computing | concurrency]] and [[Mathematical_logic | mathematical logic]]. In collaboration with the [[University_of_Joensuu | University of Joensuu]] in Finland, his group has developed the Jeliot program animation system for teaching elementary computer science and [[Computer_programming | programming]].
