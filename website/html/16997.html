{{For|the customer service practice|Housecall}} {{Merge|Trend Micro|date=September 2009}} '''HouseCall''' is a free web-based utility offered by [[Trend Micro]], which scans for and cleans computers of [[viruses]], [[Trojans]], [[spyware]], and other [[malware]]. HouseCall performs additional security checks to identify and fix vulnerabilities to prevent reinfection. According to Trend Micro HouseCall is compatible with all browsers and the following operating systems: [[Windows XP]] (32-bit) Home or Professional; Windows Vista (32-bit, 64-bit) Ultimate, Business, Home Premium, or Home Basic; and Windows 7, RC (32-bit, 64-bit).
