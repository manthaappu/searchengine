In [[modular arithmetic]], '''Barrett reduction''' is a reduction [[algorithm]] introduced in 1986 by P.D. Barrett. A naive way of computing :c = a \times b \pmod n. \, would be to use a fast [[division algorithm]]. Barrett reduction and [[Montgomery reduction]] are algorithms designed to optimize this operation. == Sources == * [[P.D.
