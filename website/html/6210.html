Biddle | | image = Christopher_Biddle.jpg | | birth_date=1963 | birth_place=[[Pennsylvania]] }} '''Christopher Columbkille Biddle''' (born October 6, 1963) ==Career== Early in his career, he worked for Bankers Trust Company New York in Global Markets out of their 130 Liberty Streethttp://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bankers_Trust_Plaza building in downtown Manhattan. In 1990, he returned to Pennsylvania to work for Professor Alexander Christakishttp://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_Christakis and Robert Whitehouse at a management consulting firm named CWA, ltd. where Chris created an AI Inference Enginehttp://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Category:Machine_learning software package for the firm which supported it's use of the Science of Generic Design to facilitate human decision making. In 1994, Chris founded Ideal Systems, Inc. which provided both business and technical expertise to Pharmaceutical, Biotech, and Medical Device manufacturers in the areas of Revenue Management, Sales Force Automation, and Finance. Ideal Systems produced several enterprise software systems to address key complex business processes within Life Sciences customers. As of 2010, these systems continue to be used by 8 of the top 10 (by revenue) life sciences manufacturers. Chris built Ideal Systems, Inc. for 12 years with annual increases in customers, revenues (gross and net), and positive EBITDA until he sold it in March 2006 to Global Healthcare Exchange, llc (GHX) - http://www.ghx.com.http://www.lexdon.com/article/Global_Healthcare_Exchange_%28GHX%29_Acquires/39985.htmlhttp://www.healthdatamanagement.com/news/13198-1.html Prior to this sale, Chris created a new firm IdealNet, Inc. to provide cross vertical business services and he is currently Chief Executive Officer there.http://www.idealnetinc.com In 2006, he founded BCGAlpha, llc which is a firm that utilizes Algorithmic Trading technology to maximize returns for customers while simultaneously minimizing risk via core risk control procedures which are implemented within both the trading systems and the firm's trading guidelines.http://www.bcgalpha.com Chris holds multiple copyrights and has authored numerous magazine articles. He holds a BA from Ursinus College and holds graduate degrees from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania as well as Trinity College Dublin.
