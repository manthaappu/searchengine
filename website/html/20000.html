{{Unreferenced|date=December 2008}} '''John E. L. Peck''' was the first permanent ''Head of Department of Computer Science'' at the [[University of British Columbia]]. He remained the Head of Department from 1969 to 1977. He was one of the editors of the original ''Report on the Algorithmic Language [[ALGOL 68]]'' and a contributing editor to the ''Revised Report on the Algorithmic Language [[ALGOL 68]]''. {{DEFAULTSORT:Peck, John E.
