'''Leslie Pack Kaelbling''' is an [[United States|American]] [[roboticist]], a professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the [[Massachusetts Institute of Technology]].[http://www.eecs.mit.edu/faculty/index.html MIT EECS faculty listing]. She was a graduate of Stanford University (Ph.D., 1990) and a student of [[Nils J. Nilsson]].[http://genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/id.php?id=69344 Mathematics Genealogy Project - Leslie Pack Kaelbling] Her research focuses on decision-making under uncertainty, [[machine learning]], and sensing with applications to [[robotics]]. She is the founder and editor-in-chief of the ''[[Journal of Machine Learning Research]]''.[http://jmlr.csail.mit.edu/editorial-board.html JMLR editorial board], retrieved 2010-01-25.
