'''Nolot''' is a [[chess]] test suite with 11 very difficult positions from real games. They were compiled by [[Pierre Nolot]] for the [[France|French]] chess magazine [[Gambisco]] and posted on the rec.games.chess [[Usenet]] group in 1994.{{cite web | url=http://groups.google.com/group/rec.games.chess/browse_frm/thread/79a6da1235df64e7?q=nolot | title=11 tactical positions computers can't solve | author=Marc-Francois Baudot | publisher=Usenet | work=rec.games.chess| date=1994-07-11 | accessdate=2007-10-13}} Some of these positions are particularly hard to solve for [[chess engine]]s. Houdini 1.03a solved problem 1 in one second however.
