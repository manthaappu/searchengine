{{notability|date=March 2010}} {{Primarysources|date=October 2007}} {{italictitle}} The '''''International Journal of Computational Cognition''''' (print: {{ISSN|1542-8060}}, online: {{ISSN|1542-5908}}) was established in 2003. It focuses on all aspects of [[cognition]]. It is also a forum for related topics including: computational verb theory, [[fuzzy logic]], [[artificial intelligence]], [[control theory]], [[robotics]], [[linguistics]] (especially physical linguistics), etc. Its founding [[editor in chief]] is [[Tao Yang (Wuxi)|Tao Yang]]. The publisher is Yang's Scientific Research Institute which is operated in both the [[United States]] and [[China]]. It is published quarterly in [[Tucson]] ([[Arizona]], USA). The [[open access journal]] is published in both online and print versions.
