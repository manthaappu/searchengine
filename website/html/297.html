The '''ACM Portal''' is an online service of the [[Association for Computer Machinery]]. [http://portal.acm.org/ ACM Portal] Its core are two main sections: '''ACM Digital Library''' ('''ACM DL'''), and the '''Guide to Computing Literature'''.[http://www.libs.uga.edu/research/subject/computer-science The University of Gerogia Guide to Online Resources] The ACM Digital Library is the full-text collection of all articles published by the ACM in its articles, magazines and conference proceedings.
