{{Infobox VG |title = |image = [[Image:The Island of Dr. Brain Coverart.png|252px]] |developer = [[Sierra Online]] |publisher = [[Sierra Online]] |designer = |engine = |version = |released = {{vgy|1992}} |genre = [[Educational game|Educational]], [[Computer puzzle game|Puzzle]] |modes = [[Single-player]] |platforms = [[DOS]] |media = [[Floppy disk]], [[CD-ROM]] |input = [[Mouse (computing)|Mouse]] }} '''''The Island of Dr. Brain''''' is the second game in the [[Dr. Brain]] series by [[Sierra Online]]. It was released in 1992 and was only available to play in DOS.{{cite news | url = http://findarticles.com/p/articles/mi_m0EIN/is_1995_March_28/ai_16719346 | title = Sierra launches the Lost Mind of Dr. Brain; New title has already achieved All Star Software Award | publisher = Business Wire | date = 1995-03-28 | accessdate = 2008-08-10}} Like the other games in the Dr. Brain series, "The Island of Dr. Brain" was an educational puzzle game. The game's story starts with an explanation by Dr. Brain. He tells the player that plans for his latest project were stolen, and he charges the player to retrieve a battery from his island and bring it to him. The player must then traverse the security puzzles Dr. Brain has set up throughout his island.{{cite web | url = http://www.abandonia.com/en/games/224/Island+of+Dr.+Brain,+The.html | title = Island of Dr. Brain, The | publisher = [[Abandonia]] | accessdate = 2008-08-10}} The game was designed by Patrick Bridgemon, and was produced and directed by Brett Miller.
