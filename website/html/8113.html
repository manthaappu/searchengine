'''Cool Water''' is a [[perfume]] introduced in 1988 by Davidoff, now a part of [[Coty,_Inc.|Coty Prestige]].Drier, Melissa [http://www.highbeam.com/doc/1G1-113469856.html Davidoff Reaches New Depths (Davidoff men's scent (Cool Water Deep))] WWD February 20 2004 retrieved 22 March 2010 The [[perfumer]] is Pierre Bourdon.[http://www.dameor.fr/en/index.php?option=com_content&view=category&layout=blog&id=45&Itemid=66] retrieved 22 March 2010. Cool Water contains a blend of [[lavender]], [[jasmine]], [[oakmoss]], [[musk]], [[sandalwood]].
