{{Outline of logic/header}} '''[[Logic]]''' is the [[formal science]] of using [[reason]]. It is considered a branch of both [[philosophy]] and [[mathematics]]. One of the aims of logic is to identify the correct (or [[validity|valid]]) and incorrect (or [[fallacy|fallacious]]) [[inference]]s. Logicians study the criteria for the evaluation of [[logical argument|arguments]]. Logic investigates and classifies the structure of statements and arguments, both through the study of [[formal system]]s of inference and through the study of arguments in [[natural language]]. The scope of logic can therefore be very large, ranging from core topics such as the study of [[fallacies]] and [[paradox]]es, to specialized analyses of reasoning such as [[probability]], correct
