{{About||the [[HIV]] drug of the same name|Didanosine}} {{Unreferenced|date=May 2007}} {{Context|date=October 2009}} '''Videx''' is a [[manufacturer]] of [[access control]] products and [[data collection]] [[Computer terminal|terminals]]. Better known in the 1980s as manufacturer of Videx VideoTerm (80 column [[display card]]) and Videx UltraTerm (80/132 column display card) add-on [[expansion card]] for [[Apple II]] computers. These products became obsolete when [[Apple Inc.|Apple]] released [[Apple IIe]] with most of the 80 column card [[hardware]] already built-in. Videx also produced software, including Desktop Calendar for the [[Apple Lisa]]. [[Category:Computer hardware companies]] [[Category:Apple II peripherals]]
